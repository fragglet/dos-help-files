─────ILINK Options──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     You can specify options in either uppercase or lowercase. For
     example, /a and /A are equivalent.
 
     Option      Action
 
     /A          ◄Checks all modules►
     /C          ◄Sets case sensitivity on►
     /E          ◄Executes commands if ILINK fails►
     /HELP       ◄Displays help►
     /I          ◄Disables full link►
     /NOLOGO     ◄Suppresses sign-on banner►
     /V          ◄Lists changed modules►
     /X          ◄Disables use of expanded memory►
     /?          ◄Displays short help►
