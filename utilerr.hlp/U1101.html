     EXEHDR error U1101
 
     automatic data segment greater than 64K; correcting heap size
 
     There was not enough space in the automatic, or default, data
     segment (DGROUP) to accommodate the requested new heap size. EXEHDR
     adjusted the heap size to the maximum available space.
 
     This error applies only to OS/2 programs.
