     Math error M6110
 
     stack overflow
 
     A floating-point expression caused a stack overflow on the
     8087/287/387 coprocessor or the emulator. Stack-overflow
     exceptions are trapped up to a limit of seven levels in addition
     to the eight levels normally supported by the 8087/287/387
     coprocessor.
 
     This error terminates the program with exit code 138.
