     HELPMAKE error H2002
 
     zero length hot spot
 
     A cross-reference was specified, but the word or anchored text
     associated with it was of zero length.
 
     With no visible text to associate with the cross-reference, the
     hot spot will be inoperative. This error is issued as a warning
     and does not prevent the building of a help file. However, some
     applications may not be able to use the resulting help file
     correctly.
