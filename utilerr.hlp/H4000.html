     HELPMAKE warning H4000
 
     keyword compression analysis table size exceeded
 
     no further new words will be analyzed
 
     The maximum number (16,000) of unique keywords has been
     encountered during keyword compression. This happens only in very
     large help files. No further keywords will be included in the
     analysis. HELPMAKE continues to analyze how frequently words occur
     that it has already encountered.
