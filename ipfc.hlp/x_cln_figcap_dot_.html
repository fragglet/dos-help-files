 :figcap.
 
The :figcap tag adds a caption to figure. The caption is a line of text that
is left-aligned in the figure. In the source file, the text must immediately
follow the tag. The tag must be used in a figure──that is, it must appear
between :fig and :efig tags.
 
This tag does not use parameters.
