 :ehpart.
 
The :ehpart tag ends the definition of cross-references for a given bitmap
or other art. The definitions must start with a :hpart tag and contain one
or more of the :ahdref, :afnref, :aspotref, :alaunch, and :ainform tags.
 
This tag does not use parameters.
