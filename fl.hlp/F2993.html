     FORTRAN compilation error F2993
 
     separator expected in format
 
     When the /4Ys compiler option is used (or the $STRICT
     metacommand is in effect), a comma (,), colon (:), right
     parenthesis ()), or slash (/) is expected to separate items
     in a format except in the following cases:
 
          ■ Between a P edit descriptor and an immediately following
            F, D, E, or G edit descriptor
 
          ■ Before or after a slash (/) edit descriptor
 
          ■ Before or after a colon (:) edit descriptor
