     FORTRAN compilation error F2420
 
     <arrayname> : ALLOCATABLE: dummy argument illegal
 
     The dummy argument, <arrayname>, was declared as an
     allocatable array.
 
     Dummy arguments may not be declared as allocatable arrays.
     Change the declaration of the dummy variable, for example,
     to static.
