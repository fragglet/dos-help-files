     FORTRAN run-time error F6983
 
     repeat count on nonrepeatable descriptor
 
     One or more BN, BZ, S, SS, SP, T, TL, TR, /, \, $, :, or
     apostrophe (') edit descriptors had repeat counts associated
     with them.
