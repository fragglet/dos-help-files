     FORTRAN compilation error F2531
 
     <name>: bound not integer
 
     The /4Ys compiler option was used in compiling (or the $STRICT
     metacommand was in effect), and a substring had a noninteger
     substring-bound expression.
 
     If the /4Ns compiler option is used in compiling (or the
     $NOTSTRICT metacommand is in effect), any arithmetic expression
     is legal and is truncated to integers through an implicit use of
     the INT intrinsic function.
