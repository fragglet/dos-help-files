     FORTRAN compilation error F2612
 
     <subprogram> : NEAR formal argument <name> : actual has FAR or HUGE
                    address
 
     It is illegal to pass an item that must be addressed with far or
     huge addressing to a formal argument that must be addressed with
     near addressing.
