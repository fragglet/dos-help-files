     FORTRAN compilation error F2206
 
     ENTRY seen before FUNCTION or SUBROUTINE
 
     An ENTRY statement appeared before any FUNCTION or SUBROUTINE
     statements in the program.
 
     An ENTRY statement can only appear in functions and subroutines.
