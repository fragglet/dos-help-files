     FORTRAN warning error F4983
 
     repeat count on nonrepeatable descriptor
 
     One or more BN, BZ, S, SP, SS, T, TL, TR, /, \, $, :, or
     apostrophe (') edit descriptors had repeat counts associated with
     them.
