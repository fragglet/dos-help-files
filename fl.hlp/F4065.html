     FORTRAN warning error F4065
 
     recoverable heap overflow in post-optimizer - some optimizations
     may be missed
 
     The compiler tried to optimize a function but ran out of memory
     while doing so. It flagged the warning, skipped the optimization,
     and continued the compilation.
 
     To avoid this problem, break the function into smaller functions.
