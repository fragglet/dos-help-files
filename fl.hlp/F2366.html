     FORTRAN compilation error F2366
 
     DOUBLE PRECISION : length specifier illegal
     DOUBLE COMPLEX   : length specifier illegal
 
     A DOUBLE PRECISION or DOUBLE COMPLEX statement included a length
     specifier. DOUBLE PRECISION is the same as REAL*8. DOUBLE COMPLEX
     is the same as COMPLEX*8.
