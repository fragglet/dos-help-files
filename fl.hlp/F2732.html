     FORTRAN compilation error F2732
 
     <stmt> : nonstandard statement
 
     (IMPLICIT NONE, INCLUDE, SELECT, END DO, DO WHILE, NAMELIST, TYPE,
     END TYPE, TYPE ( ), etc.) The statement is non-standard and
     $STRICT has been specified. ALLOCATE, DEALLOCATE TYPE, and
     INTERFACE are also nonstandard features.
