     FORTRAN compilation error F2218
 
     <name> : used or declared before ENTRY statement
 
     The name in an ENTRY statement was declared or used previously
     in the same subprogram. This caused a symbol-class conflict that
     prevented the name from being used in an ENTRY statement.
