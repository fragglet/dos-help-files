     FORTRAN compilation error F2316
 
     <name1>, <name2> : EQUIVALENCE : both in blank common block
 
     Two items specified in an EQUIVALENCE statement at different
     offsets were both in a blank common block. In the EQUIVALENCE
     statement, these items were specified to be at the same location
     in memory.
