     FORTRAN compilation error F2221
 
     <subprogram> : formal argument <name> : NEAR/FAR/HUGE mismatch
 
     The NEAR, FAR, or HUGE attributes were defined differently in the
     INTERFACE statement than in the subprogram definition or its
     arguments.
