     FORTRAN compilation error F2126
 
     <name> : automatic allocation exceeds 32K
 
     The subroutine or function <name> has an exceedingly large amount
     of compiler-generated temporary variables that take up more than
     32,767 bytes.
 
     Try splitting the subroutine or function into smaller pieces.
