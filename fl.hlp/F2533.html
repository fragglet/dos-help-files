     FORTRAN compilation error F2533
 
     <name> : lower substring bound exceeds upper bound
 
     The /4Yb compiler option was used (or the $DEBUG metacommand was
     in effect), and the value of the upper substring bound was less
     than the value of the lower substring bound.
