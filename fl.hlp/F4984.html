     FORTRAN warning error F4984
 
     integer expected preceding H, X, or P edit descriptor
 
     An integer did not precede a (nonrepeatable) H, X, or P
     edit descriptor.
 
     The correct formats for these edit descriptors are <n>H, <n>X,
     and <kP>, respectively, where <n> is a positive integer and <k>
     is an optionally signed integer.
