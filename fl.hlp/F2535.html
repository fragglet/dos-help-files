     FORTRAN compilation error F2535
 
     <name> : lower substring bound not positive
 
     The /4Yb compiler option was used (or the $DEBUG metacommand
     was in effect), and the lower substring bound was less than or
     equal to 0.
 
     The minimum value for items of type CHARACTER is 1.
