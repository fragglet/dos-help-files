     FORTRAN compilation error F2018
 
     $LINESIZE ($PACK or $PAGESIZE) : integer constant out of range
 
     An integer constant less than 80 or greater than 132 was specified
     in a $LINESIZE metacommand, or a lower bound of less than 15
     was specified in the $PAGESIZE metacommand. An integer constant
     other than 1, 2, or 4, was specified for the $PACK metacommand.
