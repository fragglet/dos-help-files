     FORTRAN warning error F4014
 
     <character> : nonalphabetic character in $DEBUG ignored
 
     A nonalphabetic character was included in the list for the
     $DEBUG metacommand.
 
     The characters a-z or A-Z are the only legal characters. Case is
     ignored.
