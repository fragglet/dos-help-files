─────Run-Time Library───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
   The following functions control opening and closing of processes:
 
   abort      _execlpe     _fatexit      raise       _spawnlpe
   atexit     _execv       _fonexit      setjmp      _spawnv
  _c_exit     _execve      _fpreset      signal      _spawnve
  _cexit      _execvp      _getpid      _spawnl      _spawnvp
  _execl      _execvpe      longjmp     _spawnle     _spawnvpe
  _execle      exit        _onexit      _spawnlp      system
  _execlp     _exit
