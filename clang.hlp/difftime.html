─────Run-Time Library───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Include:   <time.h>
 
  Syntax:    double difftime( time_t timer1, time_t timer0 );
 
  Returns:   the difference in elapsed time between <timer0> and
             <timer1>.
 
  See also:  time, clock
