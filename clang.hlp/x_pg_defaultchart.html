─────Run-Time Library───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Include:   <pgchart.h>
 
  Syntax:    short _pg_defaultchart( _chartenv __far *env,
                                     short charttype, short chartstyle );
 
             charttype: _PG_BARCHART, _PG_PIECHART, _PG_COLUMNCHART,
                        _PG_SCATTERCHART, _PG_LINECHART
 
  Returns:   a nonzero value in the case of errors, or 0 if successful.
 
  See also:  _pg_initchart, _pg_getpalette, _pg_getstyleset,
             _pg_resetpalette, _pg_resetstyleset, _pg_setpalette,
