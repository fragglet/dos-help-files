─────Run-Time Library───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Include:   <string.h>
 
  Syntax:    char *strcat( char *dest, char *src );
             char __far *_fstrcat( char __far *dest,
                                   char __far *src );
 
  Returns:   a pointer to the concatenated string.
 
  See also:  strncat, strcpy, strcmp, strlen
