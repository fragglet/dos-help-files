─────Run-Time Library───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Include:   <graph.h>
 
  Syntax:    struct _xycoord _moveto( short x, short y );
             struct _wxycoord _moveto_w( double wx, double wy );
 
  Returns:   (_moveto) the coordinates of the previous position as an
                       _xycoord structure.
             (_moveto_w) the coordinates of the previous position as a
                         _wxycoord structure.
 
  See also:  _lineto, _getcurrentposition, _setlinestyle,
             _setwritemode
