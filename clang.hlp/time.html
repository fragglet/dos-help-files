─────Run-Time Library───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Include:   <time.h>
 
  Syntax:    time_t time( time_t *timer );
 
  Returns:   the elapsed time or -1 cast to type time_t if the argument
             references a date before midnight, December 31, 1899.
 
  See also:  ctime, gmtime, localtime, _ftime, _tzset, _utime
