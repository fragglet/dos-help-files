  Constant:  RAND_MAX
 
  Include:   <stdlib.h>
 
  Context:   rand
 
  Summary:   The constant RAND_MAX is the maximum value that can be
             returned by the rand function. RAND_MAX is defined as the
             value 0x7fff.
