─────Run-Time Library───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
   The following functions control display of full-screen color text:
 
  _clearscreen         _getvisualpage        _settextcursor
  _displaycursor       _grstatus             _settextposition
  _getactivepage       _outmem               _settextrows
  _getbkcolor          _outtext              _settextwindow
  _gettextcolor        _scrolltextwindow     _setvideomode
  _gettextcursor       _setactivepage        _setvideomoderows
  _gettextposition     _setbkcolor           _setvisualpage
  _gettextwindow       _settextcolor         _wrapon
  _getvideoconfig
 
   NOTE: These functions are not available in tiny model.
