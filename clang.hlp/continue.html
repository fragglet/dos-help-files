─────C/C++ Language─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Keyword:   continue
 
  Syntax:    continue;
 
  Summary:   Passes control to the next iteration of the smallest
             enclosing do, for, or while statement in which it appears.
 
  See also:  for, while, break
