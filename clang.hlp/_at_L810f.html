─────Run-Time Library───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
   The following functions control display of user-specified fonts in
   graphics modes:
 
  _getfontinfo          _outgtext            _setgtextvector
  _getgtextextent       _registerfonts       _unregisterfonts
  _getgtextvector       _setfont
 
   NOTE: These functions are not available in tiny model.
