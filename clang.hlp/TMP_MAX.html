─────Run-Time Library───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Constant:  TMP_MAX, L_tmpnam
 
  Include:   <stdio.h>
 
  Context:   tmpnam
 
  Summary:   TMP_MAX is the maximum number of unique filenames that
             the tmpnam function can generate. L_tmpnam is the length
             of temporary filenames generated by tmpnam.
