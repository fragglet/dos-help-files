─────Run-Time Library───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Constant:  FOPEN_MAX, _SYS_OPEN
 
  Include:   <stdio.h>
 
  Summary:   Maximum number of files that can be opened simultaneously.
             FOPEN_MAX is the ANSI-compatible name. _SYS_OPEN is
             maintained for compatibility.
