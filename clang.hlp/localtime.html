─────Run-Time Library───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Include:   <time.h>
 
  Syntax:    struct tm *localtime( time_t *timer );
 
  Returns:   a pointer to a structure, or NULL if <timer> cannot be
             interpreted.
 
  See also:  gmtime, asctime, time
