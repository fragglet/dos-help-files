Outline view
 
A way of displaying a document so that the
underlying structure of the document is shown. In
outline view, you can easily rearrange large
amounts of text with just a few keystrokes. You
can also change headings and subheadings, and
make text temporarily disappear and reappear.
Outline view affects only those windows you
choose it for. See also: Body text; Collapse;
Expand; Outline organize.
