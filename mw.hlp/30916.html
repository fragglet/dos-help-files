THE DISK IS FULL AND WORD IS UNABLE TO AUTOSAVE
THE FILE.
 
■ Autosave has been cancelled. To reset the
  autosave feature, choose Utilities Customize
  and in the Frequency box, specify in minutes
  how often you want Word to automatically save
  your work.
■ To create space on the document disk, delete
  files you do not need.
■ If you are working on a floppy disk system,
  save the file to another formatted disk with
  sufficient space on it
