dGlossary
Glossary
 
The place in Word where you store text you use
repeatedly. Each piece of text you store is a
glossary entry, to which you assign a unique
name. You can save a glossary permanently in a
glossary file. Glossary files end with the
filename extension .GLY. Macros are also saved in
a glossary file. See also: Boilerplate; Macro.
