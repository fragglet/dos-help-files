DELETING A FOOTNOTE
 
Overview: <Insert Footnote command>
 
To delete a footnote:
1. Select the footnote reference mark in the
   document.
2. Choose Edit Cut (Alt,E,T), or press Del.
   The footnote mark and its accompanying text
   are deleted.
 
More Help: <Using footnotes>
 
Using Manual: Ch. 15, "Footnotes, Annotations,
              and Revision Marks"
