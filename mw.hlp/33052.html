SEARCH FOR PARAGRAPH DIALOG BOX   (Screen 1 of 3)
 
Select the paragraph formatting you want to
search for.
 
Alignment searches for:
Left:      paragraphs aligned at the left indent.
Center:    paragraphs centered between indents.
Right:     paragraphs aligned at the right indent.
Justified: paragraphs with justified text.
 
Indents searches for:
From Left:  paragraphs indented from left margin.
First Line: paragraphs with first-line indents.
From Right: paragraphs indented from right margin.
SEARCH FOR PARAGRAPH DIALOG BOX   (Screen 2 of 3)
 
Spacing searches for:
Before: a paragraph that has a specified
        number of lines preceding it.
After:  a paragraph that has a specified number
        of lines following it.
Line:   a paragraph with a specified line
        spacing.
 
Keep Paragraph searches for:
Together:  paragraphs specified not to have page
           breaks within them.
With Next: paragraphs specified not to have
           page breaks after them.
