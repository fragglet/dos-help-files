USING THE EDIT CUT COMMAND
 
Overview: <Edit Cut command>
 
To cut text or a graphic from a document:
1. Select the contents you want to remove.
2. Choose Edit Cut (Alt,E,T) or press Shift+Del.
   The selected contents are removed from the
   document and placed in the scrap.
