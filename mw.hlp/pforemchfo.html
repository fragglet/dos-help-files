REMOVING CHARACTER FORMATS
 
Overview: <Format Character command>
 
To remove character formats:
1. Select the characters whose formats you want
   to remove.
2. Do one of the following:
   ■ To remove individual formats, choose
     Format Character and turn off the
     format in the Character dialog box,
     -or-
     press the <speed formatting key> for the
     format you want to remove.
   ■ To remove all formats except font, point
     size, and color, press Ctrl+Z.
   ■ To remove all character formatting and
     restore the normal character format,
     press Ctrl+Spacebar.
 
More Help: <Formatting characters>
 
Using Manual: Ch. 6, "Text Formatting: Characters
                      and Paragraphs"
