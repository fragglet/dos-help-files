dHighlight
Highlight
 
A reverse video area on the screen that
indicates command choices, text selections, and
active command fields. For example, in Word's
normal colors, command names appear on the menus
in black text on a gray background; when a
command is selected, the text is "highlighted" by
appearing gray on a black background.
