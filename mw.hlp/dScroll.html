dScroll
Scroll
 
To move the text up, down, left, or right so that
parts of the document outside the window move
into view.
 
Use the horizontal scroll bar to move horizontally
so that parts of the document to the right or left
of the window can be viewed.
 
Use the vertical scroll bar to move vertically so
that parts of the document above or below the
window can be viewed.
