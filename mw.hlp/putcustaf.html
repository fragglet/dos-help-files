CHANGING THE AUTOSAVE FREQUENCY
 
Overview: <Utilities Customize command>
 
To turn on autosave or change the autosave
interval:
1. Choose Utilities Customize (Alt,U,U).
2. Type the autosave frequency you want (in
   minutes) in the Frequency box.
3. Turn on Confirm if you want to be prompted
   before Word automatically saves your document.
4. Choose OK.
 
More Help: <Customizing Word>
 
Using Manual: Ch. 11, "Storing Documents"
