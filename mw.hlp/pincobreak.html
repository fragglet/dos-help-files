INSERTING A COLUMN BREAK
 
Overview: <Insert Break command>
 
To insert a column break:
1. Choose Insert Break (Alt,I,B).
2. Turn on Column.
3. Choose OK.
 
More Help: <Inserting document breaks>
 
Using Manual: Ch. 7, "Page Formatting"
