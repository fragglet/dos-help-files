dCondition
Conditional instructions
 
Instructions included in a main document that
tell Word to check the text in fields of a data
document to verify that text meets certain
conditions. If the conditions are met, Word
includes the text from the field, or other text
that you specify, when it prints form documents.
Conditional instructions are also used with
macros. See also: ELSE instruction; IF
instruction; Macro; WHILE instruction.
