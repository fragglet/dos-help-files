SELECTING TEXT
 
To select:    Point to:
Word          Word, and double-click either mouse
               button
Sentence      Sentence, hold down Ctrl, and click
               the left mouse button
Line          Selection bar next to line, and
               click the left mouse button
Paragraph     Selection bar next to paragraph,
               and double-click the left mouse
               button
Document      Selection bar, hold down Ctrl, and
               click the left mouse button
