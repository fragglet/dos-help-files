SHOWING OR HIDING NON-PRINTING CHARACTERS
 
Overview: <View Preferences command>
 
You can choose to show or hide any of the
following: tabs, optional hyphens, paragraph
marks, spaces, and hidden text.
 
To show or hide any of these characters:
1. Choose View Preferences (Alt,V,E).
2. In the Non-Printing Characters box, turn the
   check box on or off for the characters you want
   to show or hide.
3. Choose OK.
 
To show all non-printing characters:
1. Choose View Preferences (Alt,V,E).
2. Turn on the Show All check box in the
   Non-Printing Characters box.
3. Choose OK.
 
More Help: <Choosing preferences>
 
Using Manual: Ch. 3, "The Word Screen"
