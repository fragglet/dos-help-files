CALCULATIONS
 
Select the mathematical expression, then press
F2, or Alt,U,C, to calculate the answer. Word
displays the result in the scrap. Choose Edit
Paste (Alt,E,P) to insert the result in a
document.
 
Operator:          Symbol:
Addition           +
Subtraction        - (or enclose number in
                    parentheses)
Multiplication     *
Division           /
Percent            % (20%*200, for example)
