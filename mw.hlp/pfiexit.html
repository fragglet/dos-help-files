ENDING A WORD SESSION
 
Overview: <File Exit Word command>
 
To end a Word session:
1. Choose File Exit Word (Alt,F,X).
2. Choose Yes to save unsaved document changes,
   No to discard unsaved changes, or Cancel.
   You return to the system prompt.
