PLACING FOOTNOTES IN A DOCUMENT
 
Overview: <Insert Footnote command>
 
To place footnotes in a document:
1. Choose Format Section (Alt,T,S).
2. To print footnotes on the same page as their
   references, turn on Same Page.
3. To print footnotes at the end of the section,
   turn on End Of Section.
4. Choose OK.
 
More Help: <Using footnotes>
 
Using Manual: Ch. 15, "Footnotes, Annotations,
              and Revision Marks"
