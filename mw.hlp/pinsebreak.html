INSERTING A SECTION BREAK
 
Overview: <Insert Break command>
 
To insert a section break:
1. Choose Insert Break (Alt,I,B).
2. Turn on Section.
3. Select where the new section begins: Next
   Page, Continuous, Even Page, or Odd Page.
4. Choose OK.
 
More Help: <Inserting document breaks>
 
Using Manual: Ch. 7, "Page Formatting"
