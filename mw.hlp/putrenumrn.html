REMOVING NUMBERS
 
Overview: <Utilities Renumber command>
 
To remove heading numbers:
1. Do one of the following:
   ■ Move the cursor to the first character or
     paragraph in the document or outline to
     remove numbers from all headings.
   ■ In <outline organize> mode, select the
     portion of the outline or document you want
     to remove numbers from.
2. Choose Utilities Renumber (Alt,U,R).
3. Select the Remove option.
4. Choose OK.
 
More Help: <Numbering paragraphs and outlines>
 
Using Manual: Ch. 24, "Outlining"
