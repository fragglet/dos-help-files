VIEW FOOTNOTES/ANNOTATIONS
 
Procedures: <Viewing Footnotes and Annotations>
 
Use the Footnotes/Annotations command to display
the <footnotes>/<annotations> pane.
 
■ The footnotes/annotations pane displays only the
  footnotes and annotations with <reference marks>
  visible in the document window.
 
■ As you scroll through the document window, the
  footnotes/annotations pane scrolls to show the
  corresponding footnotes and annotations.
 
■ The Footnotes/Annotations command is unavailable
  if the current document does not contain any
  footnotes or annotations.
 
■ To move the cursor between the document window
  and the footnotes/annotations pane, press F6.
