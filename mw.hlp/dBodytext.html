dBodytext
Body text
 
In outline view, text that is not a heading and
has no level assigned to it. Word displays body
text with all assigned formatting, instead of
suppressing paragraph formats as it does with
headings. In outline view, body text appears with
a "T" next to it in the selection bar. See also:
Outline view.
