DRAWING LINES
 
Overview: <Utilities Line Draw command>
 
To draw lines:
1. Choose Utilities Line Draw (Alt,U,L).
   LD (line draw) appears in the status bar.
2. Use the direction keys and the Home and End
   keys to draw lines.
3. Press Esc or click the left mouse button to
   turn off Line Draw.
 
More Help: <Using line draw>
 
Using Manual: Ch. 21, "Line Drawing"
