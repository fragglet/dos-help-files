UTILITIES RENUMBER
 
Procedures: <Numbering paragraphs and outlines>
 
Use the Utilities Renumber command to
automatically number or renumber headings in
<outline view> or renumber paragraphs in
text view.
 
■ You can renumber an entire outline or a selected
  portion of an outline.
 
■ You can also use this command to remove
  paragraph or heading numbers.
 
■ The Renumber command does not number headings
  that begin with a hyphen(-), asterisk(*), or
  bullet(■).
