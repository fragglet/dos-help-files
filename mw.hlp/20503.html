WORD IS EXITING LAYOUT VIEW.
 
Word ran out of memory while displaying your
document in layout view.
 
1. Choose File Close All.
2. Choose File Open and open the document again.
3. Try View Layout again to view the remainder of
   your document, or divide the document in half
   before choosing View Layout.
