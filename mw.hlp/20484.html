DO YOU WANT TO BACK UP CHANGES WITH AUTOSAVE NOW?
 
Word is ready to back up changes you have made to
files that are open.
 
■ To back up the files, choose OK.
■ To skip the autosave, choose Cancel.
■ For autosave to occur without displaying this
  message, choose Utilities Customize and turn
  off the Confirm check box.
