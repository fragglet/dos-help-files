IF instruction
 
In a main document, an instruction that tells Word
to verify that text in data fields meets certain
conditions. If the conditions are met, Word
includes the text from the field, or other text
that you specify, when it prints form documents.
IF instructions may also be used in macros. See
also: ELSE instruction; Form document; Macro;
Main document.
