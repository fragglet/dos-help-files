dSort
Sort
 
To automatically rearrange selected text based on
numeric or alphanumeric criteria.
 
A numeric sort includes digits and the following
characters commonly associated with digits: $, %,
- (minus sign), , (comma), ( ) (parentheses), and
. (decimal point). The text is arranged in either
ascending (1, 2, 3 ...) or descending (3, 2, 1)
order.
 
An alphanumeric sort includes letters, mixed
letters and numbers, or numbers arranged
uniformly with special characters, such as dates
in yy/mm/dd format.
