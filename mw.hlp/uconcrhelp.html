USING HELP CONCURRENTLY WITH YOUR DOCUMENT
 
You can work on your document with Help displayed
at the same time on the screen. For example,
you could arrange the Help window below your
document window, find the procedure in Help
for formatting a paragraph, and then format a
paragraph in your document using the Help
instructions.
 
To use Help concurrently with your document:
1. Position the cursor in your document.
2. Press F1 to get Help.
3. Size the document window and Help window so
   that they appear side by side or above and
   below each other.
   To position the Help window below your
   document, use the Window Arrange All
   command.
4. You can now move back and forth between the
   document and Help windows, browsing Help for
   the topic you want and then returning to your
   document to apply what you learn.
