REPLACING A WORD WITH A SYNONYM
 
Overview: <Utilities Thesaurus command>
 
To replace a word with a synonym:
1. Select or place the cursor on the word you
   want to find a synonym for.
2. Choose Utilities Thesaurus (Alt,U,T).
3. Select the synonym you want from the Synonyms
   or Definitions list.
4. Choose Replace.
 
More Help: <Using the thesaurus>
 
Using Manual: Ch. 30, "Thesaurus"
