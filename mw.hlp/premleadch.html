REMOVING LEADER CHARACTERS
 
Overview: <Format Tabs command>
 
To remove leader characters:
1. Position the cursor in the paragraph
   containing the leader characters you want to
   remove, or select a group of paragraphs.
   Note: If you select a group of paragraphs,
         only the tab stops common to all
         paragraphs will be shown on the ruler
         or in the dialog box.
2. Choose Format Tabs (Alt,T,T).
3. In the Tab Position box, select the tab you
   want to remove the leader character from.
4. Under Leader, select the None option.
5. Choose the Set button.
6. Repeat steps 3-5 for each tab stop containing
   the leader characters you want to remove.
7. Choose OK.
 
More Help: <Working with tabs>
