MOVING AROUND IN HELP
 
You move from topic to topic in Help by choosing
jumps. Jumps are links between Help topics, and
are available in any Help topic Word displays in
the Help <window>. You can jump to a definition
of a word or phrase, to another Help topic such
as a procedure or command, or to a Learning Word
lesson or demonstration.
 
Jumps are enclosed in angle brackets < >. When
you select a jump, the angle brackets are
<highlighted>.
 
To choose a jump:
■ Using the mouse, click anywhere between the
  angle brackets.
  -or-
■ Using the keyboard, press the Tab key or the
  first letter of the word in brackets to select
  the jump, and then press Enter.
 
To move between jumps in a topic:
■ Press Tab to move to the next jump.
■ Press Shift+Tab to move to the previous jump.
 
Note: Any jump that begins "Lesson:" or "Demo:"
      takes you to the related Learning Word
      tutorial lesson or demonstration. When you
      exit Learning Word, you return to Help.
