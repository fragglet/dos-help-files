CHANGING WORD'S UNIT OF MEASURE
 
Overview: <Utilities Customize command>
 
The default unit of measure in Word is inches.
 
To change Word's unit of measure:
1. Choose Utilities Customize (Alt,U,U).
2. From the Measure list, select the measurement
   unit you want (In, Cm, P10, P12, Pt).
3. Choose OK.
 
More Help: <Customizing Word>
