COPYING TEXT OR GRAPHICS
 
Overview: <Edit Copy command>
 
To copy text or graphics to the scrap:
1. Select the text or graphics you want to copy.
2. Choose Edit Copy (Alt,E,C) or press
   Ctrl+Ins.
   A copy of the selected contents is now saved
   in the scrap.
