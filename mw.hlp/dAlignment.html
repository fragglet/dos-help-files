dAlignment
Alignment
 
The horizontal position of text within the width
of a line or between tab stops. Text can be
aligned all the way to the left, all the way to
the right, or in the center of each line or
between tab stops. Text can also be justified to
fill the line. In Word, alignment can be an
attribute of paragraphs or tab stops. See also:
Justified; Ragged.
