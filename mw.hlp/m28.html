WINDOW OPEN WINDOWS
 
Procedures: <Activating a window>
 
The list of currently open windows is displayed at
the bottom of the Window menu.
 
■ Open windows are listed in the order in which
  they were opened.
 
■ You can have up to nine document windows open
  simultaneously, in addition to the Help window.
 
■ The active document in the open windows list
  is indicated by a mark (∙) in front of the
  document name.
 
■ To open the next or previous window in the open
  windows list, press Ctrl+PgUp or Ctrl+PgDn.
