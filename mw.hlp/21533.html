PRINTING WAS INTERRUPTED.
DO YOU WANT TO CONTINUE PRINTING?
 
Word displays this message if you press Esc
during printing or if your printer is not ready.
If you printer is not ready, make sure it is
online and the hardware is properly connected.
 
■ To continue printing, choose OK.
■ To stop printing, choose Cancel.
