PRINT OPTIONS DIALOG BOX          (Screen 1 of 2)
 
Options
  Choose Draft for a quick printout, if you do
  not want to print with microspace justification
  or graphics. Word leaves an empty space where
  the graphic is positioned. Choose Duplex to
  print on both sides of the page.
 
Include
  Turn on Summary Info and/or Hidden Text to
  print additional data with your document.
PRINT OPTIONS DIALOG BOX          (Screen 2 of 2)
 
Graphics Resolution
  Select a printer resolution that corresponds to
  your printer model. The choices available
  depend on the printer designated in the Printer
  Setup dialog box.
