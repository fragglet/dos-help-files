MAXIMIZING A WINDOW
 
Overview: <Window Maximize command>
 
To enlarge a window to its maximum size:
 
Using the mouse:
■ Click the Maximize icon (the double-headed
  arrow), in the upper-right corner of the
  window border.
 
Using the keyboard:
■ Choose Window Maximize (Alt,W,X) or press
  Ctrl+F10.
 
More Help: <Window Restore command>
           <Window Size command>
