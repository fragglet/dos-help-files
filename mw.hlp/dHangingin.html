dHangingin
Hanging indent
 
When the first line of a paragraph extends to the
left of the remainder of the paragraph. Common
usages of the hanging indent are bulleted or
numbered lists. To create a standard hanging
indent, place the cursor in the paragraph in
which you want a hanging indent and press Ctrl+T.
Use the Format Paragraph command to create a
customized hanging indent.
