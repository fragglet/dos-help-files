THE SCRAP IS EMPTY.
 
To cut and paste text or graphics:
1. Select the text or graphics you want to move
   or copy.
2. Choose Edit Cut or Edit Copy. This inserts the
   selected text or graphics into the scrap.
3. Move the cursor to the location at which you
   want to insert the text or graphics.
4. Choose Edit Paste.
