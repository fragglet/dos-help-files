RENUMBERING HEADINGS
 
Overview: <Utilities Renumber command>
 
To renumber all or part of an outline in outline
view:
1. Remove any numbers from the original heading
   levels by selecting the Remove option in the
   Utilities Renumber dialog box, or by deleting
   the numbers manually.
2. Select the first character or heading in the
   outline to renumber the entire outline, or
   switch to <outline organize> mode and select
   the portion of the outline you want to
   renumber.
3. Choose Utilities Renumber (Alt,U,R).
4. Select the All option.
5. Do one of the following:
   ■ Turn on Restart Sequence to number from the
     first number in a sequence; for example,
     1 or i.
   ■ Turn off Restart Sequence to number from
     the current position in a sequence; for
     example, 3 or iii.
6. Choose OK.
