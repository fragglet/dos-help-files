MOVING TEXT OR GRAPHICS
 
Overview: <Edit Cut command>
 
To move text or graphics within a document:
1. <Select> the text or graphics you want to
   move.
2. Press Shift+Del to <cut> the selected
   contents or Ctrl+Ins to <copy> the selected
   contents. Either action places the selected
   text or graphics in the scrap.
3. Place the cursor where you want to insert the
   scrap contents.
4. Press Shift+Ins.
   The scrap contents are inserted in front of
   the cursor.
 
To move text or graphics between documents:
1. Open the document you want to move the text
   or graphics from.
2. Select the contents you want to move.
3. Press Shift+Del to cut the selected
   contents or Ctrl+Ins to copy the selected
   contents.
4. Open the document you want to move the text or
   graphics to.
5. Move the cursor to where you want to insert
   the scrap contents.
6. Press Shift+Ins.
   The contents of the scrap are inserted in
   front of the cursor.
