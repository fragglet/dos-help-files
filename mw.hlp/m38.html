LEARNING WORD
 
<Learning Word Menu>
 
Begins the tutorial lessons for learning to use
Word. If you use a floppy disk system, or do not
have the lessons installed on your hard drive,
Word will prompt you to insert the appropriate
Learning Word disk.
 
To proceed directly to the lessons:
■ Press Enter or click the Learning Word Menu
