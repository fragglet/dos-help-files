MARK REVISIONS DIALOG BOX         (Screen 1 of 2)
 
Mark Revisions
  Turn the revision marks command on or off.
 
Revision Bars
  Select where you want word to print revision
  bars. If you select Outside, Word prints
  revision bars in the outside margin on facing
  pages. Word only displays revision bars in the
  left margin.
 
Mark New Text With
  Specify the format you want for new text.
MARK REVISIONS DIALOG BOX         (Screen 2 of 2)
 
Search
  Choose Search to find the next occurrence of
  revised text.
 
Accept Revisions
  Choose Accept Revisions to delete strikethrough
  text and revision bars, and accept revised
  text.
 
Undo Revisions
  Choose Undo Revisions to delete revised text
  and revision bars, and remove strikethrough
  formatting from text.
