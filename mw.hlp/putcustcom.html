SHOWING KEY COMBINATIONS ON MENUS
 
Overview: <Utilities Customize command>
 
The default setting for this option is to show
key combinations.
 
To display or hide key combinations:
1. Choose Utilities Customize (Alt,U,U).
2. Turn Show Keys On Menus on or off.
3. Choose OK.
 
More Help: <Customizing Word>
