EDITING SHORTCUTS
 
The following procedures are shortcuts for
copying and moving text and for copying
formatting. Always select text before you
copy or move it.
 
To:                     Point to:
Copy selected text      New destination, press
                         and hold Ctrl+Shift,
                         and click the right
                         mouse button
Move selected text      New destination, press
                         and hold Ctrl, and
                         click the right mouse
                         button
Copy character          Character with desired
 formatting to           format, press and hold
 selected text           Ctrl+Shift, and click
                         the left mouse button
Copy paragraph          Selection bar next to
 formatting to           paragraph with desired
 selected text           format, press and hold
                         Ctrl+Shift, and click
                         the left mouse button
