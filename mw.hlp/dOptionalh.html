dOptionalh
Optional hyphen
 
A hyphen you insert to indicate where you want to
break a word if the word needs to be divided at
the end of a line. Optional hyphens are invisible
until they are needed (or until you turn on the
Optional Hyphens option with the View Preferences
command).
