dCutandpas
Cut and paste
 
The technique of creating or editing documents by
cutting selected text or a graphic from a
document and pasting it in another location in
the document, or in a different document. In
Word, "cutting" refers to removing text from a
document into the scrap; "pasting" refers to
inserting that text into a desired location. Use
the Edit Cut and Edit Paste commands to cut and
paste. See also: Copy; Scrap; Select.
