VIEWING FOOTNOTES AND ANNOTATIONS
 
Overview: <View Footnotes/Annotations command>
 
To open a footnotes/annotations pane:
■ Choose View Footnotes/Annotations.
  Word marks the Footnotes/Annotations command
  on the menu when the footnotes/annotations pane
  is open.
 
To format footnotes and annotations:
■ Format text and reference marks in the
  footnotes/annotations pane as you would format
  text in a document window.
 
■ For information on how to change the
  <automatic style> of a footnote, see
  Chapter 28, "Style Sheets."
 
■ To move the cursor between the document window
  and the footnotes/annotations pane, press F6.
 
■ To close the footnotes/annotations pane:
  Choose View Footnote (Alt,V,F).
