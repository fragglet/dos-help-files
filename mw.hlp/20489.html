DETACHING STYLE SHEET. DO YOU WANT TO CONVERT
STYLE SHEET FORMATTING TO DIRECT FORMATTING?
 
When you detach a style sheet, you can keep the
style formatting or you can convert it to direct
formatting.
 
■ To convert style formatting to direct
  formatting, choose Yes.
■ If you do not want to keep style formatting in
  the Word document, choose No.
  Note: If you choose No, you can still reapply
        the styles by reattaching the style
        sheet.
