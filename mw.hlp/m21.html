UTILITIES THESAURUS
 
Procedures: <Using the thesaurus>
 
Use the Utilities Thesaurus command to display a
list of definitions for a word and synonyms for
those definitions.
 
■ You can choose to see a list of synonyms and
  definition words for any synonym or definition
  word listed.
 
■ If the thesaurus cannot find the word you
  selected, it displays a list of alternative
  words that are alphabetically similar.
 
■ When you replace a selected word with a
  synonym, Word maintains the same
  capitalization, punctuation, and format as the
  selected word.
