REMOVING PARAGRAPH BORDERS OR SHADING
 
Overview: <Format Borders command>
 
To remove paragraph borders or shading:
1. Move the cursor into the bordered or shaded
   paragraph.
2. Choose Format Borders (Alt,T,B).
3. Do one of the following:
   ■ To remove all border lines, select None.
   ■ To remove only a specific border line, turn
     off the corresponding border line check box
     (Top, Bottom, Left, or Right).
   ■ To remove shading, select 0 in the
     Percentage box.
4. Choose OK.
 
More Help: <Adding paragraph borders and shading>
