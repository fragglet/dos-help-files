THE SELECTION IS VERY LARGE. DO YOU WANT TO
FORMAT THE ENTIRE SELECTION AS A HEADER OR
FOOTER?
 
Check your text selection to verify that you want
to format it as a header or a footer.
 
■ To create the header or footer, choose Yes.
■ To cancel the command, choose No.
  To create a header or a footer, select the
  text you want and choose Format Header/Footer
  again.
