REMOVING A BOOKMARK
 
Overview: <Insert Bookmark command>
 
To remove a bookmark:
1. Choose Insert Bookmark (Alt,I,M).
2. Select the bookmark you want to delete from
   the Names box.
3. Choose the Delete button.
4. Choose OK.
 
More Help: <Working with bookmarks>
 
Using Manual: Ch. 13, "Bookmarks and
              Cross-Referencing"
