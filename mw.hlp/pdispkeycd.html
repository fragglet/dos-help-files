DISPLAYING KEY CODES IN THE STYLE BAR
 
Overview: <View Preferences command>
 
To display key codes in the <style bar>:
1. Choose View Preferences (Alt,V,E).
2. In the Show box, turn on the Style Bar check
   box.
3. Choose OK.
 
More Help: <Working with styles and style sheets>
 
Using Manual: Ch. 28, "Style Sheets"
