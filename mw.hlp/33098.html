HYPHENATE DIALOG BOX              (Screen 1 of 1)
 
Hyphenate At
  Shows where Word proposes to split the word to
  be hyphenated. Use the direction keys to move
  the division point.
 
Confirm
  Turn on Confirm if you want Word to prompt you
  to confirm each proposed hyphenation. You can
  use the direction keys to change Word's proposed
  division point.
 
Hyphenate Caps
  Turn on Hyphenate Caps if you want to hyphenate
  words containing capital letters.
