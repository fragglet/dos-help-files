dScrollbar
Scroll bar
 
The right window border contains the vertical
scroll bar; the bottom window border contains the
horizontal scroll bar. You use the vertical
scroll bar to scroll up and down in the document.
If the text in the document is wider than the
window, use the horizontal scroll bar to see text
to the right or left of the window. The scroll
bars can be turned off and on with the View
Preferences command.
