UTILITIES SPELLING
 
Procedures: <Spelling procedures>
 
Use the Utilities Spelling command to check and
correct spelling and check for misplaced
punctuation marks.
 
■ When you check spelling, Word automatically
  suggests corrections for unknown words.
 
■ You only have to correct a misspelled or unknown
  word once. Thereafter, the corrected word will
  be automatically corrected each time it is
  found.
 
■ Word has three types of dictionaries. You can
  add:
  * Words you use frequently to the standard
    dictionary.
  * Words associated with a group of documents to
    a user dictionary.
  * Words associated with a specific document to
    a document dictionary.
