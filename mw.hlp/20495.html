DO YOU WANT TO UPDATE THIS LINK?
CHOOSE YES TO UPDATE OR NO TO GO ON.
 
You asked Word to update links from your document
to another file or files. You decide which links
to update and which to skip.
 
■ To update the link, choose Yes.
■ To keep the link as it is and continue to the
  next link, choose No.
■ To stop updating links, choose Cancel.
