DISPLAYING .D. AND .L. CODES
 
Overview: <Insert File command>
 
To display .D. and .L. codes:
1. Choose View Preferences.
2. In the Non-Printing Characters box, turn on
   Hidden Text.
3. Choose OK.
   All hidden text, including .D. and .L. codes,
   appears in the document window.
