SEARCHING FOR REVISION MARKS
 
Overview: <Utilities Revision Marks command>
 
To search for revised text:
1. Choose Utilities Revision Marks (Alt,U,M).
2. Choose the Search button.
   Word scrolls to, and highlights, the next
   occurrence of revised text.
