WORD CANNOT MOVE TEXT INTO ITSELF.
 
You selected text and then attempted to insert it
within the selected area in your document.
 
1. Select the text you want to move or copy.
2. Move the cursor to the new location that is
   outside of the selected area.
3. Press Ctrl and click the right mouse button.
