CHANGING CHARACTER CASE
 
Overview: <Format Character command>
 
To change character case:
1. Select the characters you want to change.
2. Press Shift+F3 until the characters have the
   case you want.
 
Note: Word cycles through case in this order:
      lowercase, uppercase, and then first letter
      uppercase.
 
More Help: <Formatting characters>
 
Using Manual: Ch. 6, "Text Formatting: Characters
                      and Paragraphs"
