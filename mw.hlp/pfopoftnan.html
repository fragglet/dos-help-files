PLACING AND PRINTING FOOTNOTES AND ANNOTATIONS
 
Overview: <Format Section command>
 
To position footnotes and annotations:
1. Choose Format Section (Alt,T,S).
2. If you want to print footnotes or annotations
   on the page they refer to, turn on Same Page.
3. If you want to print footnotes or annotations
   at the end of the section, turn on End Of
   Section.
4. Choose OK.
 
More Help: <Formatting a section>
 
Using Manual: Ch. 15, "Footnotes, Annotations,
                       and Revision Marks"
