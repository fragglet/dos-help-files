30785
THERE IS NO BITMAP GRAPHIC IN THE WINDOWS
CLIPBOARD.
 
The graphic you want to insert is missing from
the Windows Clipboard or is in a format that
cannot be used in Word documents.
 
1. Exit Word temporarily by pressing Alt+Tab.
2. Verify that the text or graphic you want to
   insert in the Word document is located in the
   Windows Clipboard.
3. Return to Word and choose Insert Picture
   again.
