DELETING AND INSERTING TEXT
 
To:                          Press:
Copy selection to scrap      Ctrl+Ins or Alt+F3
Cut selection to scrap       Shift+Del
Delete character above       Del
 cursor
Delete character to left     Backspace
 of cursor
Erase selection              Del
 permanently
Insert glossary entry        Type glossary entry
                              name, then press F3
Insert text from scrap       Shift+Ins
Toggle overtype and insert   Ins
 mode
Undo last action             Alt+Backspace
 
Note:  Choose the Utilities Customize command to
       turn on the Use INS For Overtype Key
       option.
