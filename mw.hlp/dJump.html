dJump
Jump
 
In online Help, text enclosed in angle brackets
( < > ) "jumps" you to another Help topic. There
are two types of jumps: definition jumps display a
definition of the word or words, without leaving
the current Help topic; all other jumps leave the
current topic and display the contents of the
Help topic enclosed in the angle brackets.
