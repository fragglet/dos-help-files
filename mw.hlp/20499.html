DO YOU WANT WORD TO CONTINUE TO HYPHENATE FROM
THE BEGINNING OF THE DOCUMENT?
 
Word has hyphenated the document from the cursor
position to the end.
 
■ To hyphenate from the beginning of the document
  to the cursor position, choose Yes.
■ If you don't want to hyphenate the document
  above the cursor position, choose No.
