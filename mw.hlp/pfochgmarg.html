CHANGING THE MARGINS
 
Overview: <Format Margins command>
 
To change the <margins> in a document:
1. Choose Format Margins (Alt,T,M).
2. In the Top, Bottom, Left, and/or Right boxes,
   type new measurements.
3. Choose OK.
 
More Help: <Formatting margins>
 
Using Manual: Ch. 7, "Page Formatting"
