26650
WORD CANNOT READ ONE OF THE DOWNLOADED FILES.
 
Word does not recognize the format of one of the
font files you attempted to download to the
printer.
 
■ Recopy the file from a disk, or recreate the
  font file using the appropriate software.
■ Check your fonts against the original.
■ Reinstall the fonts.
■ Look in the Printer Manual to see if Word
  supports your fonts.
