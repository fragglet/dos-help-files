THE TEXT IS NOT FOUND IN THE SELECTION.
 
The text you typed in the Search dialog box does
not occur within the passage you have selected.
 
1. Word searches only within the selected text.
   If you want to search the entire document or
   all text after the cursor, turn off the
   selection.
2. Choose Edit Search again and check your typing
   in the Search box.
3. To search again, choose OK.
