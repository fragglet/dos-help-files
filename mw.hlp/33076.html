INTERRUPTING A MACRO              (Screen 1 of 1)
 
When you press Esc while a macro is running, you
have three options:
 
■ If you want to stop running the macro, choose
  Cancel.
 
■ If you want to continue to run the macro one
  step at a time (in order to observe its
  operations more carefully), turn on the Single
  Step option and choose OK.
 
■ If you want to continue running the macro,
  choose OK.
