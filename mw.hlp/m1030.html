INSERT BREAK
 
Procedures: <Inserting document breaks>
 
Use the Insert Break command to end a page,
column, or <section> at the cursor location.
Breaks in a Word document appear as dotted lines
on the screen.
 
■ A Section break affects the text that precedes
  it. The new section is the text before the
  break, not after it.
 
■ Select, delete, move, or copy breaks as you
  would any other characters.
 
■ To quickly insert a column break, press
  Ctrl+Shift+Enter.
