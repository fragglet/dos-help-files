DO YOU WANT TO ATTACH A STYLE SHEET TO THIS
FILE?
 
You chose File Open to open an RTF document in
Word. If the RTF document includes styles, you
can preserve them by attaching an existing style
sheet or creating a new one during the conversion
from RTF to Word. If you do not attach a style
sheet, Word converts the styles to direct
formatting.
 
■ To attach a style sheet to the RTF document and
  preserve formatting, choose Yes.
■ To convert style formatting to direct
  formatting, choose No.
