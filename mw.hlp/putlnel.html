ERASING LINES
 
Overview: <Utilities Line Draw command>
 
To erase lines drawn with the Line Draw command:
1. Press Esc to turn off Line Draw.
2. Select the portion of the line or lines you
   want to delete.
3. Press Del.
 
More Help: <Using line draw>
 
Using Manual: Ch. 21, "Line Drawing"
