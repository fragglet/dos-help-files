INSERT A SHEET OF PAPER IN THE PRINTER.
 
Word has finished printing one page of your
document.
 
■ To continue printing, insert another page in
  your printer and choose OK.
■ If you use form-feed paper or your printer has
  a sheet feeder, you can print without pausing
  at the end of each page. Choose File Printer
  Setup and select Paper Feed: Continuous.
