dCOMMENTin
COMMENT instruction
 
A macro instruction used to place comments in the
text of a macro. These comments might describe
the purpose of a group of commands and
instructions, the operation of the macro, or
serve as notes for editing the macro. Comments do
not affect how the macro runs.
