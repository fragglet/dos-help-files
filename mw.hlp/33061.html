RENUMBER DIALOG BOX               (Screen 1 of 1)
 
Renumber Paragraphs
  All     Select to renumber paragraphs or
          headings.
  Remove  Select to remove paragraph or heading
          numbers.
 
Restart Sequence
  Turn on Restart Sequence to number from the
  first number in a sequence; for example, 1 or i.
 
  Turn off Restart Sequence to number from the
  current position in a sequence; for example, 3
  or iii.
