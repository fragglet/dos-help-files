INSERTING TEXT OR GRAPHICS
 
Overview: <Edit Paste command>
 
To insert text or graphics from the scrap:
1. Place the cursor where you want to insert
   the scrap contents.
2. Choose Edit Paste (Alt,E,P) or press
   Shift+Ins.
   The contents of the scrap will be inserted in
   front of the cursor.
