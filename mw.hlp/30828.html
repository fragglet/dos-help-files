30828
THE DISK IS FULL OR WRITE-PROTECTED, OR THE DRIVE
IS EMPTY. WORD CANNOT CREATE THE FILE.
 
■ Use the CHKDSK command to make sure the disk is
  formatted.
■ Make sure the disk is correctly inserted in the
  disk drive, and the drive door is closed.
■ Remove the write-protect tab from the disk.
