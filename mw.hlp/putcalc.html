CALCULATING AN EXPRESSION
 
Overview: <Utilities Calculate command>
 
To calculate an expression:
1. Type the expression in the document.
2. Select the expression.
3. Choose Utilities Calculate (ALT,U,C) or
   press F2.
   Word calculates the expression and places the
   result in the scrap.
 
Using Manual: Ch. 23, "Math"
