Footnote
 
Text placed at the bottom of a page or at the end
of a document or chapter to give a reference for,
or add information about, a topic or term
discussed in the main text of the document.
 
Footnotes are created with the Insert Footnote
command. You can view and edit footnotes and
annotations in the footnote window. See also:
Annotation.
