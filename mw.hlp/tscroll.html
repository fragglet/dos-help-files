SCROLLING
 
Scrolling key combinations are the same in Word
5.0 and Word 5.5, with the following exceptions:
 
                       Word 5.0    Word 5.5
To move cursor:        Press:      Press:
To beginning of        Ctrl+PgUp   Ctrl+Home
 document
To end of document     Ctrl+PgDn   Ctrl+End
To top of window       Ctrl+Home   Ctrl+PgUp
To bottom of window    Ctrl+End    Ctrl+PgDn
