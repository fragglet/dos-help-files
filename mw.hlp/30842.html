PAGINATION IS REQUIRED.
 
You chose Edit Go To and specified a page number
before your document was paginated.
 
■ Choose Utilities Repaginate Now to number the
  pages.
■ Choose Utilities Customize and select
  Background Pagination to break pages
  automatically as you work.
■ Choose Edit Go To and request the page number
  you want to move to.
