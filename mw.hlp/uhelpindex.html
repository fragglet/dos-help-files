USING THE HELP INDEX
 
The Help Index provides a list of the categories
of Help topics:
 
 Procedures       Step-by-step instructions about
                  how to perform specific tasks.
 Commands         Descriptions of menu commands
                  with <jumps> to related
                  procedures and commands.
 Keyboard         Word keystrokes.
 Mouse            Tips on using the mouse.
 Screen Regions   Descriptions of the different
                  regions of the Word screen.
 Word 5.0 to 5.5  Changes from Word 5.0 to 5.5,
                  including comparisons of command
                  names, keystrokes, and mouse
                  procedures.
 Definitions      Word 5.5 terminology.
 
To browse the Help Index:
1. Choose Help Index (Alt,H,I) or press F1
   while the cursor is in the document window.
2. Choose the category of Help you want.
3. Choose the topic you want to view.
 
To return to the Help Index:
■ Choose Index at the top of the Help window.
  -or-
■ Choose Back or press Backspace to backtrack
  through the Help topics.
