RETRY ACCESS TO THIS FILE? IF YOU CANCEL, YOU
WILL EXIT WORD AND LOSE ALL EDITS.
 
Word displays this message for any of several
reasons. Before you continue:
 
■ Make sure the filename is typed correctly.
■ Confirm that you have properly inserted the
  correct, formatted disk in the correct drive.
■ Remove the write-protect tab on the disk.
■ If, after correcting these problems, you still
  get this message, try your backup copy. Your
  disk could be damaged.
