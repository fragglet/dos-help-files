SCROLLING
 
You can scroll continuously by holding down the
left mouse button when you click scroll arrows
or the scroll bar.
 
You must have scroll bars turned on to use these
procedures. See the <View Preferences command>.
 
To scroll:            Do this:
Up or down one line   Click up or down scroll
                       arrow
Left or right one     Click left or right scroll
 character             arrow
Up or down one        Click above or below scroll
 screen                box on vertical scroll bar
Left or right         Click left or right of
 one-third of a        scroll box on horizontal
 screen                scroll bar
To relative           Drag the scroll box to
 vertical              position relative to
 position              percentage of document's
                       length
To relative           Drag the scroll box to
 horizontal            position relative to
 position              percentage of document's
                       width
To start or end of    Drag the scroll box to top
 document              or bottom of vertical
                       scroll bar
