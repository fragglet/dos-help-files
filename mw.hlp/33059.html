THESAURUS DIALOG BOX              (Screen 1 of 2)
 
Not Found...Choose Another Word
  Displays the word selected in your document. The
  thesaurus cannot find any synonyms for this
  word. Word displays words that are
  alphabetically close to the word you selected.
 
Replace
  Replaces the selected word in your document with
  the word selected in the Choose Another Word
  list box.
THESAURUS DIALOG BOX              (Screen 2 of 2)
 
Synonyms
  Displays synonyms for the word selected in the
  Choose Another Word list.
 
Original
  Goes back to the original word you looked up.
