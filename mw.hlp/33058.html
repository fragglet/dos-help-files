THESAURUS DIALOG BOX              (Screen 1 of 2)
 
Synonyms For
  Displays the selected word, or you can type a
  word you want to look up.
 
Definitions
  Displays definitions for the selected word.
 
Synonyms
  Displays synonyms for the word selected in the
  Definitions or Synonyms list.
THESAURUS DIALOG BOX              (Screen 2 of 2)
 
Replace
  Replaces the word selected in your document with
  the word selected in the Definitions or Synonyms
  list.
 
Synonyms
  Displays synonyms for the word selected in the
  Synonyms list.
 
Original
  Goes back to the original word you looked up.
