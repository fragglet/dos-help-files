Topic Command
Shortcut Key:  F1
 
Use the Topic command for help on a specific topic. Place
the cursor anywhere on the topic you want to look up, or on
the space following it. The topic may be in your program or
in a QuickC help file.
 
QuickC displays the topic in the menu:
 
Topic: xxxxxxxx   F1
           ▲
           └───── First 8 letters of topic to look up
 
TIP:  Press F1 anywhere in the QuickC environment to
      search for help on the topic under the cursor.
