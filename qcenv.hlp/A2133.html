     Error: A2133
     can't order alphabetically with one-pass assembly
 
     The /a option requires two-pass assembly. Specify two-pass
     assembly (/P2) and try assembling again. (Within the environment,
     turn off the One Pass Assembly option in the Assembler Flags
     dialog box.)
