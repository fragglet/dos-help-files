n.40
You made changes to your program, then entered a Run
(F5), Trace Into (F8), or Step Over (F10) command before
saving the changes.
 
Choose <Yes> to save your changes, and rebuild the program
before proceeding with the command.
 
Choose <No> to carry out the command with the original,
unchanged version of the file.
