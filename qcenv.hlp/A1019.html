     Error: A1019
     Error defining symbol from command line: 'symbol name'
 
     This message indicates that assembly was terminated because you
     gave an invalid name in a symbol definition. (Symbol definitions
     may be given with the QCL /D option or from the Assembler Flags
     dialog box.) The name may be a reserved word or include illegal
     punctuation.
