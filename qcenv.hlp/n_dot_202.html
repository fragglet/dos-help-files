n.202
You executed part of your program during a debugging
session, then interrupted the session to do something
else (for example, rebuild your program). You will
need to start execution from the beginning of the
program. QuickC automatically starts the program the
next time you enter a Run (F5), Trace Into (F8), or Step
Over (F10) command.
