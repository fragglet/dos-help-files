    The index lists help topics alphabetically. You will see an
    "alphabet bar" at the top of each screen in the index. From
    the alphabet bar, you can jump to other parts of the index.
 
    To select index topics with the Mouse:
 
      1. Move the cursor over a letter on the alphabet bar.
      2. Click Right on the letter.
 
    To select index topics with the Keyboard:
 
      1. Press a letter on the keyboard to jump to the corresponding
         letter on the alphabet bar.
      2. Press ENTER.
 
    (You can also press the TAB key to move to the letter on the
    bar.)
 
    Once at an alphabetical listing for help topics, choose a
    topic with the mouse, or use the TAB key to move to the topic
    and press ENTER.
