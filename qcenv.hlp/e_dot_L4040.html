     Warning: L4040
     Stack size ignored for /TINY
 
     You specified a stack segment for a program in COM format. To
     avoid errors, the linker ignores the stack-segment definition.
     Although a stack segment is required for most programs, you
     should avoid defining a stack segment when generating a COM file.
