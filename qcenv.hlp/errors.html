    When QuickC or QuickAssembler encounters a compiler, assembler,
    linker, or run-time error, they open the error window at the
    bottom of the screen.
 
    To get help on errors with the Mouse:
 
      1. Move the cursor to the error message line in the Error window.
      2. Click Right on the line.
 
    To get help on errors with the Keyboard:
 
      1. Move the cursor to the error message line in the Error window.
      2. Press F1.
