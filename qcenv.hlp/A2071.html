     Error: A2071
     Forward needs override or FAR
 
     A call or jump attempted to access a far label that was not
     declared far earlier in the source code. You can use the PTR
     operator to specify far calls and jumps, as shown below:
 
            call    FAR PTR task
            jmp     FAR PTR location
