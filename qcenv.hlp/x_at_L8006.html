    The table of contents lists the major sections of the help
    system. It is a good starting point for exploration of the
    help system. Use any of the following methods to select a
    topic:
 
      ■ Press the TAB key until the screen cursor touches the
        desired topic. Then press F1.
 
      ■ Press the first letter of the desired topic until the
        screen cursor touches it. Then press F1.
 
      ■ Move the mouse cursor to the desired topic and click
        Right.
