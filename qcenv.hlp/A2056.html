     Error: A2056
     Immediate mode illegal
 
     An immediate operand was supplied to an instruction that cannot
     use immediate data. For example, the following statement is
     illegal:
 
            mov    ds,DGROUP
 
     You must move the segment address into a general register and
     then move it from that register to DS.
