     Error: A2057
     Illegal size for operand
 
     You attempted to use an instruction with a register of the wrong
     size, as in the following example:
 
            jmp    ah
 
     The assembler cannot resolve such an instruction, because the
     size of a register is absolutely fixed. In the example above, you
     could correct the instruction by replacing AH with AX.
