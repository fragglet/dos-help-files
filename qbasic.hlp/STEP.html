STEP Keyword
  ◄Contents►  ◄Index►  ◄Back►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
In a FOR...NEXT loop, specifies how much to increase the counter in each
iteration. In graphics statements, specifies that pixel coordinates are
relative to the current graphics cursor position.
