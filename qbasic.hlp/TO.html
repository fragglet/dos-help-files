Specifies ranges for:
 
    ■ A CASE clause of a SELECT CASE statement.
    ■ A loop counter in a FOR...NEXT loop.
    ■ Records to lock or unlock in a LOCK...UNLOCK statement.
    ■ Lower and upper bounds in a DIM or REDIM statement.
