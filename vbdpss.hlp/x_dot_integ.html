  ■ Integrated Introduction to Computing
    By Donald J. Weinshank, Mark Urban-Lurain and Thomas Danieli
    Kendall/Hunt Publishing Company
    Ordering info:  Al Grisanti 1-319-589-1081 or 1-319-589-1114
 
    This is a national introductory computer science telecourse targeted
    to non-majors. The course integrates TV lectures, textbook, sample
    problems, and instructional materials, using an innovative "CALL before
    write" approach in which the first keyword which students see is CALL.
    This is followed by SUB, FUNCTION, loops, and CASE. I/O via Forms is
    introduced about the middle of the term.
