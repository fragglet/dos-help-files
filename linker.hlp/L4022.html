     LINK Warning L4022
 
     'group1', 'group2' : groups overlap
 
     The given groups overlap. Since a group is assigned to a physical
     segment, groups cannot overlap in segmented executable files.
 
     Reorganize segments and group definitions so the groups do not
     overlap. Refer to the map file.
