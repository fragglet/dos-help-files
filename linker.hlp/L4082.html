     LINK Warning L4082
 
     changing overlay assigment for segment 'segment' from 'oldnumber'
     to 'newnumber'
 
     The given segment was assigned to two overlays, represented by
     <oldnumber> and <newnumber>. LINK assumed the <newnumber> overlay.
 
     Probably a command-line overlay specification with parentheses
     conflicted with an overlay specification in the module-definition
     (.DEF) file.
