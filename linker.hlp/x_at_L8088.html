─────EXEHDR─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     For an application, gives the application's starting address.
 
     For a DLL, gives the address of the initialization routine. If the
     DLL has no initialization routine, the start address is zero.
 
     Displayed for segmented executable files.
