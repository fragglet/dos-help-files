     Syntax:  /S[TACK]:number
 
     Sets the stack allocation to <number> bytes. This option is
     equivalent to LINK's /STACK option.
     See: ◄LINK /STACK Option►
 
     Specify <number> in decimal format or C-language notation.
     See: ◄Entering Numeric Arguments►
