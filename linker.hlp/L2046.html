     LINK Error L2046
 
     share attribute conflict - segment 'segment' in group 'group'
 
     The given segment has a different sharing attribute than other
     segments that are assigned to the given group.
 
     All segments assigned to a group must have the same attribute,
     either SHARED or NONSHARED. The attributes cannot be mixed.
