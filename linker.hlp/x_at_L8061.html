/HEAP Option
                                             ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
─────EXEHDR─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     Syntax:  /HEA[P]:number
 
     Sets the heap allocation field to <number> bytes. This option is
     only for segmented executable files.
 
     Specify <number> in decimal format or C-language notation.
     See: ◄Entering Numeric Arguments►
