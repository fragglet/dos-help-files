TITLE BAR
 
■═════════════ DOCUMENT NAME ════════════════↕
 └────┬─────────────────────────────────────┘
      └─ Title bar
 
The title bar displays the document name. The
document name is "Document X" until you save the
document under a new name.
 
You can reposition the window on the Word screen
by dragging the title bar or the line that forms
the left window margin.
