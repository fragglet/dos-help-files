SPLIT ICON
                                      ══════════↕
                              Split icon ──────►═
                                                ┴
                                                ↑
Double-click the split icon to divide the       █
window into two panes. This enables you to see  ▒
two different parts of the same document.
 
You can reposition the split by dragging the
split icon up or down.
 
To undo the split, double-click the icon again.
