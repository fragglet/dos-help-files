MAXIMIZE ICON
 
                     ══ DOCUMENT NAME ══════════↕
                                                ▲
                                 Maximize icon ─┘
 
Click the maximize icon to enlarge a document
window to its maximum size. Click the icon again
to return the window to its original size.
 
The letters MX are displayed in the status bar
when you maximize a window.
