   └─ Ribbon
 
Use the ribbon to change styles, fonts,
font sizes, and character formatting. To see a
list of the choices you have for each option,
click the down arrow next to that option.
 
You can make text bold, italic, or underlined by
clicking one of the options available on the
right end of the ruler.
 
Click the ruler/ribbon icon (┴) with the right
mouse button to display or hide the ribbon.
