  Constant:  _PG_LEFT, _PG_CENTER, _PG_RIGHT, _PG_BOTTOM, _PG_OVERLAY
 
  Include:   <pgchart.h>
 
  Context:   Justify member of titletype structure.
             Place member of legendtype structure.
 
  Synopsis:  _PG_LEFT, _PG_CENTER, and _PG_RIGHT specify how the title
             will be justified within the chart window.
 
             _PG_RIGHT, _PG_BOTTOM, and _PG_OVERLAY specify how the
             legend will be placed within the data window.
