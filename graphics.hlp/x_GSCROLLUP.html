  Constant:  _GSCROLLUP, _GSCROLLDOWN
 
  Include:   <graph.h>
 
  Context:   _scrolltextwindow
 
  Synopsis:  Specifies direction in which to scroll text.
 
     The text-scrolling constants and their corresponding actions are
     as follows:
 
     Constant         Action
 
     _GSCROLLUP       Scroll up one line
     _GSCROLLDOWN     Scroll down one line
