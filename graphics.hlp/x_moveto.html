  Include:   <graph.h>
 
  Syntax:    struct xycoord _moveto( short x, short y );
             struct _wxycoord _moveto_w ( double wx, double wy );
 
  Returns:   (_moveto) the coordinates of the previous position as an
                       xycoord structure.
             (_moveto_w) the coordinates of the previous position as an
                         _wxycoord structure.
