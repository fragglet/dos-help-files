  void Revoke();
 
  Remarks
 
  Call this function to revoke (shut down) a client document. Because
  Revoke is called by the COleClientDoc destructor, it is seldom called
  explicitly elsewhere. Revoke may be called with no ill effects for a
  document that has already been shut down.
 
  Prior to the document Revoke call, you must call COleClientItem::Release
  or COleClientItem::Delete for each client item contained within the
  document.
