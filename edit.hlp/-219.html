Your current document either has never been saved or has
not been saved since it last changed. Either:
  ■ Choose <Yes> to save the document, including changes.
    If the document is not named, you will be prompted to
    give it a filename.
  ■ Choose <No> to discard your changes. If your document
    is new and has never been saved, <No> will discard
    the entire document.
  ■ Choose <Cancel> to cancel the command, and return to
    your document.
