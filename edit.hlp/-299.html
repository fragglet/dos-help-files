Exit Command
Use to leave the MS-DOS Editor environment.
 
If your document has changed since you last saved it, the
MS-DOS Editor displays a dialog box.
  ■ Choose <Yes> to save your document, including changes.
  ■ Choose <No> to discard your changes.
  ■ Choose <Cancel> to cancel the Exit command and return
    to your document.
