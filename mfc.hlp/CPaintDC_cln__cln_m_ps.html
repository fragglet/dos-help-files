  Remarks
 
  m_ps is a public member variable of type ◄PAINTSTRUCT►. It is the
  PAINTSTRUCT that is passed to and filled out by ◄CWnd::BeginPaint►.
 
  The PAINTSTRUCT contains information that the application uses to paint
  the client area of the window associated with a CPaintDC object.
 
  NOTE: You can access the device-context handle through the PAINTSTRUCT.
        However, you can access the handle more directly through the m_hDC
        member variable, which CPaintDC inherits from CDC.
