  void operator &=( const RECT& rect );
 
  Parameter   Description
 
  <rect>      Contains a RECT or CRect.
 
  Remarks
 
  Sets CRect equal to the intersection of CRect and <rect>. The
  intersection is the largest rectangle contained in both rectangles.
