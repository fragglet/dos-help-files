  void Cut();
 
  Remarks
 
  Deletes (cuts) the current selection (if any) in the edit control and
  copies the deleted text to the Clipboard in CF_TEXT format.
 
  The deletion performed by Cut can be undone by calling the Undo member
  function.
 
  To delete the current selection without placing the deleted text into
  the Clipboard, call the ◄Clear► member function.
