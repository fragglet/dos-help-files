  CDialog();
 
  Remarks
 
  The CDialog constructor is protected because you must derive your own
  dialog-box class to implement a dialog.
 
  Construction of a modeless dialog is a two-step process. First invoke
  the constructor, then call either form of the Create member function.
  You can combine the steps by calling Create from within your
  constructor.
