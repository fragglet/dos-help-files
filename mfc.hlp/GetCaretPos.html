  static CPoint GetCaretPos();
 
  Remarks
 
  Retrieves the client coordinates of the caret's current position, and
  copies them to a CPoint structure.
 
  The caret position is given in the client coordinates of the CWnd
  window.
 
  Return Value
 
  CPoint containing the coordinates of the caret's position.
