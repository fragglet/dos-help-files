  void Cut();
 
  Remarks
 
  Deletes (cuts) the current selection (if any) in the combo-box edit
  control, and copies the deleted text onto the Clipboard in ◄CF_TEXT►
  format.
 
  To delete the current selection without placing the deleted text onto
  the Clipboard, call the ◄Clear► member function.
