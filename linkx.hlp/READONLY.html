─────<readonly> Attribute───────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     Used by:  DATA, SEGMENTS
 
     Determines access rights to a data segment.
 
     Keyword       Purpose
 
     READONLY      Specifies that the segment can only be read.
 
     READWRITE     Default. Specifies that the segment is both readable
                   and writeable.
