─────/NOI Option────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     Syntax:  /NOI[GNORECASE]
 
     The /NOI option preserves case in identifiers. By default, LINK
     treats uppercase and lowercase letters as equivalent.
 
     Use /NOI when linking programs written in case-sensitive languages
     such as C.
