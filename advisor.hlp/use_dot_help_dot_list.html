─────Microsoft Advisor──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     Some topics are not a screen of text with fixed hyperlink buttons
     at the top. Instead, they are a list of topics in which each line
     is a hyperlink. The entire line is highlighted at a time.
 
     To activate a hyperlink line:
 
        ■  Point to the line and click the right mouse button.
 
       OR
 
       ■  Press the UP ARROW and DOWN ARROW keys to select a topic.
          Then press F1 or ENTER to go to that topic.
