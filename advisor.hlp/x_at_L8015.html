What Is a Link?
                                             ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
─────Microsoft Advisor──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     A link is a word or phrase, that when chosen, brings up a screen
     that gives more information on that topic. A link can appear
     anywhere on a Help screen.
 
     There are two types of links in the Help system: links identified
     by triangles and links to language keywords and functions (which
     are not surrounded by triangles).
