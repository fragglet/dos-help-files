     Drop-Down Lists
 
     Some dialog boxes contain a drop-down list:
 
     ┌─Drop-Down List───┐
     │[Item 1·········]↓│
     └──────────────────┘
 
     To select an item in the list, press the ARROW keys until the item
     appears in the box.
 
     To open the list, click the highlighted arrow or press ALT+DOWN.
     The opened list appears as follows:
 
     ┌─Drop-Down List───┐
     │[Item 1·········]↓│
     └┌────────────────┐┘
      │Item 1          ↑
      │Item 2          █
      │Item 3          ▒
      │Item 4          ↓
      └────────────────┘
 
     To select an item from the list, click it or press the ARROW keys.
     The list works like other lists.
     See: ◄Selecting Items from a List►
 
     To close the list box, click the highlighted arrow or press ALT+UP.
