     Select Items from a List
 
     You can select items from a list to change directories and drives,
     you can also select those items you do or do not want from a list
     box.

     of the dialog box
 
     Select the double dots (..) to move up one directory level.
 
     To select items from a list box:
 
     Mouse:
        ■ Click the arrows or drag the scroll box to move through
          the list of drives and directories.
        ■ Double-click the directory or drive you want.
 
     Keyboard:
        ■ Press TAB to move the cursor into the list box
        ■ Use the ARROW keys to highlight your selection.
        ■ Press ENTER.
