hypot, hypotl
 ◄Description► ◄Example►                     ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Include:   <math.h>, <errno.h>
 
  Syntax:    double hypot( double x, double y );
             long double hypotl( long double x, long double y );
 
  Returns:   the length of the hypotenuse if successful, or HUGE_VAL
             on overflow.
             errno: ERANGE
 
  See also:  cabs
