strstr, _fstrstr
 ◄Description► ◄Example►                     ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Include:   <string.h>
 
  Syntax:    char *strstr( char *string1, char *string2 );
 
             char _far *_fstrstr( char _far *string1,
                                  char _far *string2 );
 
  Returns:   a pointer to the first occurrence of <string2> in
             <string1>, or NULL if <string2> is not found.
 
  See also:  strcspn, strncat, strncmp, strncpy, strnicmp, strpbrk,
             strrchr, strspn
