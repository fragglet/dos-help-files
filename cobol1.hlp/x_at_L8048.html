  Verb:         EXIT
 
  Function:     Marks the end of a procedure or cycle of an in-line
                perform, causing the indicated level of procedure (PERFORM
                CYCLE, PERFORM, PARAGRAPH, SECTION or PROGRAM) to be
                terminated at that point.
