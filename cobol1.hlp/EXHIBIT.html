────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Verb:         EXHIBIT
 
  Function:     Displays data items, optionally preceded by their
               identifiers, and literals. The display can be conditional.
 
  Syntax:
                                    ┌──────────────┐
                                    ▼              │
     ►►───EXHIBIT─┬─NAMED─────────┬─┬─identifier-1─┬────►◄
                  ├─CHANGED NAMED─┤ └─literal-1────┘
                  └─CHANGED───────┘
                                    -♦-
