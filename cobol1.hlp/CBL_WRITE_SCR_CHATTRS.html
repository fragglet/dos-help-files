  Routine:      CBL_WRITE_SCR_CHATTRS
 
  Function:     Writes a string of characters and their attributes
                to the screen.
 
  Syntax:       CALL "CBL_WRITE_SCR_CHATTRS" USING     screen-position
                                                       character-buffer
                                                       attribute-buffer
                                                       string-length
                                             RETURNING status-code

                  column-number          PIC X COMP-X.
                character-buffer       PIC X(n).
                attribute-buffer       PIC X(n).
                string-length          PIC X(2) COMP-X.
