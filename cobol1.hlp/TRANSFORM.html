  Verb:         TRANSFORM
 
  Function:     Replaces occurrences of a character or group of
                characters in a data item.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─TRANSFORM identifier-3 CHARACTERS──────►
 
     ►──FROM─┬─figurative-constant-1─┬─TO─┬─figurative constant-2─┬───►
             ├─nonnumeric-literal-1──┤    ├─nonnumeric-literal-2──┤
             └─identifier-1──────────┘    └─identifier-2──────────┘
                                    -♦-
