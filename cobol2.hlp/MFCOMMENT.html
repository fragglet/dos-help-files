────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Directive:    MFCOMMENT
 
  Function:     Prevents lines with an asterisk (*) in column 1, or a
                form-feed character in columns 1 and 2 followed by an
                asterisk, appearing in the source listing.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──MFCOMMENT─────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      MFCOMMENT
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Any
