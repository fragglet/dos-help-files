────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Switch:       M
 
  Function:     Makes the COMMAND.COM transient area available for use
                by the application.
 
  Warning!      Setting this switch prevents the last command entered
                from being saved in the buffer; therefore, you will be
                unable to use the standard DOS command line editing
                facility.
 
  Default:      Off
