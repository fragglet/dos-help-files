  Directive:    COPYLIST
 
  Function:     Makes the compiler list the contents of files named
                in COPY statements.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──COPYLIST─┬───────────┬────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘           └─"integer"─┘
 
  Parameters:   integer              Must be 0 or between 50 and 99.
                                     The segment-number
 
  Default:      COPYLIST
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Any
