  Directive:    REPORT-LINE
 
  Function:     Specifies the maximum length of a report writer line.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──REPORT-LINE──"integer"────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   integer              The maximum length, in characters.
 
  Default:      REPORT-LINE "256"
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Initial
