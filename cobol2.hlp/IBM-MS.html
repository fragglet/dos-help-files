  Directive:    IBM-MS
 
  Function:     Specifies that words reserved in IBM COBOL 1.00
                are to be regarded as reserved words, and changes
                the behavior of certain features to be compatible
                with that product.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──IBM-MS────────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOIBM-MS
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Initial
