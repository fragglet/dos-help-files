  Directive:    STICKY-LINKAGE
 
  Function:     Makes parameters to the program remain linked during
                subsequent calls of the program.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬──────STICKY-LINKAGE──"integer"─┬─►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO───STICKY-LINKAGE────────────┘
 
  Parameters:   integer              Either 1 or 2
 
  Default:      NOSTICKY-LINKAGE
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Initial
