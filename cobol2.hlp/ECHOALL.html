  Directive:    ECHOALL
 
  Function:     Sends a full listing to the screen as well as to a
                printer or other device specified with the LIST or
                PRINT directive.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──ECHOALL───────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOECHOALL
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         No
