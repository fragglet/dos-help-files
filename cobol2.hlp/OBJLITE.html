  Directive:    OBJLITE
 
  Function:     Produces a version of your program that functions very
                much like an assembler subroutine.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──OBJLITE───────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOOBJLITE
 
  Phase:        Generate
 
  $SET:         Initial
