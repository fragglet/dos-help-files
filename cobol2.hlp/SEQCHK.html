  Directive:    SEQCHK
 
  Function:     Makes the compiler check the sequence numbers in
                columns 1 through 6 and identify source lines that
                are out of sequence.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──SEQCHK────────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOSEQCHK
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Any
