  Directive:    TABLESEGCROSS
 
  Function:     Makes the compiler assume that a table defined as not
                crossing a segment boundary may, in fact, cross a segment
                boundary.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──TABLESEGCROSS─────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOTABLESEGCROSS
 
  Phase:        Generate
 
  $SET:         Initial
