  Directive:    HOST-NUMCOMPARE
 
  Function:     Controls the operation of comparisons between integer
                numeric data items of USAGE DISPLAY and alphanumeric
                literals or figurative constants.

     ►►─┬───┬─┬─────HOST-NUMCOMPARE────────┬────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO──HOST-NUMCOMPARE────────┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOHOST-NUMCOMPARE
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Any
