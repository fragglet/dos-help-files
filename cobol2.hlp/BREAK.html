  Directive:    BREAK
 
  Function:     Sets a breakpoint at the specified paragraph or
                section.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬─────BREAK──"proc-name"─────┬────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO──BREAK──────────────────┘
 
  Parameters:   proc-name            Name of a paragraph or section in
                                     the program being animated.
 
  Default:      NOBREAK
