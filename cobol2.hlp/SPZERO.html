────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Directive:    SPZERO
 
  Function:     Causes space characters in numeric data items of USAGE
                DISPLAY to be treated as zeros in native code.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──SPZERO────────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOSPZERO
 
  Phase:        Generate
 
  $SET:         Initial
