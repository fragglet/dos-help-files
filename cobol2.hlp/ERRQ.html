ERRQ (↑ Compiler Control)
 ◄Key►                                       ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Directive:    ERRQ
 
  Function:     Makes the compiler ask, each time it gives an error
                message, whether you want to stop compiling.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──ERRQ──────────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOERRQ
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Any
