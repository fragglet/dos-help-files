  Directive:    FP-ROUNDING
 
  Function:     Determines whether one floating-point receiving item
                can affect the results of other, nonfloating-point
                receiving items.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬─────FP-ROUNDING──"dialect"───┬──►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO──FP-ROUNDING──────────────┘
 
 
  Parameters:   dialect              Must be VSC2 or OSVS.
 
  Default:      NOFP-ROUNDING
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Any
