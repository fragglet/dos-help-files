  Directive:    WB
 
  Function:     This directive is used with Micro Focus integrated

                integration. Do not change its setting.

     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──WB────────────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOWB
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         No
