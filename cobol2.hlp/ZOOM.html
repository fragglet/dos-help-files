  Directive:    ZOOM
 
  Function:     Invokes Animator in zoom mode
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬───────ZOOM──"program-name"─┬────►◄
        └─/─┘ └┬────┬─ZOOM─────────────────┘
               └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   program-name         Zoom until this subprogram
                                     is entered.
 
  Default:      NOZOOM
