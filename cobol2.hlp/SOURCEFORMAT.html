     ►►─┬───┬─SOURCEFORMAT──"format-type"───────►◄
        └─/─┘

                                     COBOL source.
 
  Default:      SOURCEFORMAT"FIXED"
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Any
