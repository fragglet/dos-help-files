  Directive:    SUPFF
 
  Function:     Suppresses form-feed characters on the compilation
                list if it is sent to the screen.

     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──SUPFF─────────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      SUPFF
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Any
