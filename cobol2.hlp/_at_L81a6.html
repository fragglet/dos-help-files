     The possible values of block mode are:
 
      ALL      Blocking occurs for fetch-only cursors. Ambiguous cursors
               are treated as fetch-only.
 
      UNAMBIG  Blocking occurs for fetch-only cursors. Ambiguous cursors
               are treated as updatable.
 
      NO       Blocking does not occur.
 
     This directive only affects IBM Database Manager.
