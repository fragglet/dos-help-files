────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Directive:    REMOVE
 
  Function:     Removes words from the reserved word list, so that they
                can be used as user-defined words.
 
  Syntax:
                      ┌──────────┐
                      ▼          │
     ►►─┬───┬──REMOVE──"rsv-word"───────────────►◄
        └─/─┘
 
  Parameters:   rsv-word              A reserved word.
 
  Default:      No reserved words are removed.
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Initial
