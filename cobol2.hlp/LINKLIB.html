LINKLIB (↑ Compiler Control)
 ◄Key► ◄Details►                             ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Directive:    LINKLIB
 
  Function:     Specifies the link library or libraries where the
                linker is to look for the Run-Time Support routines.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬─────LINKLIB──"library-names"─┬──►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO──LINKLIB──────────────────┘
 
  Parameters:   library-names        The names of one or more
                                     link libraries.
 
  Default:      LINKLIB "COBLIB+COBAPI"
 
  Phase:        Generate
 
  $SET:         Initial
