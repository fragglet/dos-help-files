  Directive:    OPTSIZE
 
  Function:     Makes the compiler treat the size of the object
                program as a higher priority than its speed, within
                the constraints imposed by other directives.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬───OPTSIZE─────────────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      OPTSIZE
 
  Phase:        Generate
 
  $SET:         Initial
