  Directive:    FLASH-CALLS
 
  Function:     Makes Animator display the user screen at every
                CALL statement and whenever a PERFORM statement is
                stepped over.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──FLASH-CALLS───────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOFLASH-CALLS
