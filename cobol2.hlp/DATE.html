DATE (↑ Compiler Control)
 ◄Key► ◄Details►                             ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Directive:    DATE
 
  Function:     Puts the date in the DATE-COMPILED paragraph and at
                the top of each page of the listing.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬───────DATE──"string"─┬──────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └┬────┬─DATE───────────┘
               └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   string               An alphanumeric literal.
 
  Default:      DATE
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         No
