  Directive:    SMALLDD
 
  Function:     Tells the compiler that no Linkage Section record less
                than 64K long crosses a segment boundary.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──SMALLDD───────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      SMALLDD
 
  Phase:        Generate
 
  $SET:         Initial
