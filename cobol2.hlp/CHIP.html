  Directive:    CHIP
 
  Function:     Tells the compiler that a specific microprocessor
                architecture is in use.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬─────CHIP──"integer"─┬───────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO──CHIP────────────┘
 
  Parameters:   integer              Must be 16.
 
  Default:      CHIP "16"
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Initial
