────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Directive:    OPTSPEED
 
  Function:     Makes the compiler treat the speed of the object
                program as a higher priority than its size, within the
                constraints imposed by other directives.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──OPTSPEED──────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOOPTSPEED
 
  Phase:        Generate
 
  $SET:         Initial
