  Directive:    RESEQ
 
  Function:     Makes COBOL line sequence numbers appear on Animator's
                display of the source.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──RESEQ─────────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NORESEQ
