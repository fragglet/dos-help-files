  Directive:    MAPNAME
 
  Function:     Makes the compiler alter program-names and entry-points
                to make them compatible with OS/VS COBOL, DOS/VS COBOL,
                VS COBOL II and COBOL/370.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──MAPNAME───────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOMAPNAME
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Initial
