RETRYLOCK (↑ Choosing Run-time Behavior)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Directive:    RETRYLOCK
 
  Function:     Specifies that when a READ statement finds that a
                record is locked the read operation is to be re-tried
                repeatedly until the record is available.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──RETRYLOCK─────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NORETRYLOCK
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Any
