────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     When shift-out and shift-in characters are specified by this
     directive, every DBCS literal must have the shift-out character
     immediately after the opening quotation mark and the shift-in
     character immediately before the closing quotation mark.
 
     They act as additional delimiters to the literal, and are not
     part of its value. With NODBCSSOSI, no shift-out and shift-in
     characters are needed or recognized.
 
     This feature may not be supported by your compiler.
