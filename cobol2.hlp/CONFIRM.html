  Directive:    CONFIRM
 
  Function:     Makes the compiler echo all subsequent directives to
                the screen.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──CONFIRM───────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOCONFIRM
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         No
