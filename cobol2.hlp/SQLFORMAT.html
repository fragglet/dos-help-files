  Directive:    SQLFORMAT
 
  Function:     Determines the date and time format when date/time fields
                are assigned to string representations in host variables.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬──SQLFORMAT──"date-format"─────────►◄
        └─/─┘
 
  Parameters:   date-format          Identifies the format to use.
 
  Default:      SQLFORMAT "LOC"
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Initial
