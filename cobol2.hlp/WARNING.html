  Directive:    WARNING
                WARNINGS
 
  Function:     Specifies the lowest severity level of errors to report.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬─WARNING──┬─"integer"─┬─────►◄
        └─/─┘ │    └─WARNINGS─┘           │
              └─NO─┬─WARNING──┬───────────┘
                   └─WARNINGS─┘
 
  Parameters:   integer              1, 2, or 3.
 
  Default:      WARNING "1"
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Any
