  Directive:    DOSVS
 
  Function:     Specifies that words reserved in IBM DOS/VS COBOL are
                to be treated as reserved words.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──DOSVS─────────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NODOSVS
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Initial
