  Directive:    RTNCODE-SIZE
 
  Function:     Specifies the size of the RETURN-CODE special register
                and its alignment in memory.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬──RTNCODE-SIZE──"integer"──────────►◄
        └─/─┘
 
  Parameters:   integer              2 or 4.
 
  Default:      RTNCODE-SIZE "2"
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Initial
