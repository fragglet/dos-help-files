────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Directive:    ASMLIST
 
  Function:     Makes the compiler produce an assembly listing.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬──┬──────ASMLIST─┬─"file-name"─┬───►◄
        └─/─┘  │              └─()─────────┬┘
               └┬────┬ASMLIST──────────────┘
                └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   file-name            Listing file. Can include a path.
 
  Default:      NOASMLIST
 
  Phase:        Generate
 
  $SET:         Initial
