  Directive:    FORM
 
  Function:     Specifies the number of lines on each page of
                the listing.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬─────FORM──"integer"─┬───────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO──FORM────────────┘
 
  Parameters:   integer              Must be greater than 3.
 
  Default:      FORM "60"
 
  Phase:        Both
 
  $SET:         Any
