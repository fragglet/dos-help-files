  Directive:    NCHAR
 
  Function:     Enables the use of Micro Focus Double-Byte Language
                Extension (PIC N, Japanese data-names and Japenese
                procedure-names).
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬───────NCHAR──"integer"─┬────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └┬────┬─NCHAR────────────┘
               └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   integer              Must be 1 or 2. The level of support
                                     required.
 
  Default:      NOJAPANESE
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Initial
