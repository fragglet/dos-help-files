────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Directive:    FLAG-CHIP
 
  Function:     Makes the compiler produce flags indicating potential
                problem areas on the chip architecture in use.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──FLAG-CHIP─────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOFLAG-CHIP
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Any
