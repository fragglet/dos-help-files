────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     When OLDBLANKLINE is specified, the BLANK LINE clause behaves
     exactly the same as BLANKEOL; that is all characters to the right of
     the cursor are deleted.
 
     With NOOLDBLANKLINE specified, the BLANK LINE clause causes the
     whole line to be deleted.
