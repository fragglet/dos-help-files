  Directive:    REGPARM
 
  Function:     Makes the object program use an alternative
                parameter-passing mechanism, used in some other COBOL
                compilers.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬───────REGPARM──"when-used"─┬────►◄
        └─/─┘ └┬────┬─REGPARM──────────────┘
               └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   when-used              IN or OUT.
 
  Default:      NOREGPARM
 
  Phase:        Generate
 
  $SET:         Initial
