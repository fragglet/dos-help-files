────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Directive:    STDERR
 
  Function:     Causes error messages to be echoed to STDERR rather
                than to the console (STDOUT).
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──STDERR──────────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOSTDERR
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Any
