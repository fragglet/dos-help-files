  Directive:    64KSECT
 
  Function:     Makes the compiler produce segment breaking information
                at each paragraph instead of each section, for programs
                containing sections that produce more than 64K of code.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──64KSECT───────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Paramters:    None
 
  Default:      NO64KSECT
 
  Phase:        Generate
 
  $SET:         Initial
