  Directive:    SETTING
                SETTINGS

                list of the settings of directives.

     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬─┬─SETTING──┬───────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘ └─SETTINGS─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOSETTING
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         No
