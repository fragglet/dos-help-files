  Directive:    FLAGSINEDIT
 
  Function:     Specifies whether flagging messages are to be included
                in an error file.

     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──FLAGSINEDIT───────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      FLAGSINEDIT
 
  Phase:        Both
 
  $SET:         No
