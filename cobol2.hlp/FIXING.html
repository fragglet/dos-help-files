────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Directive:    FIXING
 
  Function:     Tells the compiler how many times to perform its final
                pass which shortens jump instructions.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬──FIXING──"integer"────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘
 
  Parameters:   integer               Must be between 1 and 99.
 
  Default:      FIXING "99"
 
  Phase:        Generate
 
  $SET:         Any
