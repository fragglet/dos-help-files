JAPANESE (↑ Allowing Language Features)
 ◄Key► ◄Details►                             ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Directive:    JAPANESE
 
  Function:     Enables the use of Micro Focus Japanese Language
                Extension (PIC N, Japanese data-names and Japanese
                procedure-names).
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬───────JAPANESE──"integer"─┬─────►◄
        └─/─┘ └┬────┬─JAPANESE────────────┘
               └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   integer              Must be 1 or 2. The level of support
                                     required.
 
  Default:      NOJAPANESE
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Initial
