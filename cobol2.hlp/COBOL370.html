  Directive:    COBOL370
 
  Function:     Specifies that words reserved in IBM COBOL/370 are
                to be treated as reserved words, and allows features
                selectively for compatibility with a given level of
                that product.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬───────COBOL370──"integer"─┬─────►◄
        └─/─┘ └┬────┬─COBOL370────────────┘
               └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   integer              Must be 1. The level of IBM
                                     COBOL/370 to be compatible with.
 
  Default:      NOCOBOL370
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Initial
