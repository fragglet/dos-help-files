  Directive:    CASE
 
  Function:     Prevents external symbols (such as PROGRAM-ID and names
                of called programs) being converted to upper case.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──CASE──────────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOCASE
 
  Phase:        Generate
 
  $SET:         Any
