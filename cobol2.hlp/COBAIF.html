  Directive:    COBAIF
 
  Function:     Specifies whether to load and save Animator session
                information.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬───────COBAIF──"path-name"──┬────►◄
        └─/─┘ └┬────┬─COBAIF───────────────┘
               └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   path-name            The location of the Animator
                                     information file.
 
  Default:      COBAIF
