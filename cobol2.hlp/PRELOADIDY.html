  Directive:    PRELOADIDY
 
  Function:     Causes Animator to load all .IDY files, even for programs
                only running in zoom mode. This ensures that all breakpoints
                being carried over to this session remain active.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──PRELOADIDY────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      PRELOADIDY
