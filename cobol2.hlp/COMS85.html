  Directive:    COMS85
 
  Function:     Allows your program to contain syntax introduced in
                the Communications Module of the ANS85 COBOL Standard.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──COMS85────────────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOCOMS85
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Initial
