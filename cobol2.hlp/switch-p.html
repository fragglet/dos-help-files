────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Switch:       P
 
  Function:     Causes the DOS shared Run-Time System to perform its
                printer I/O by calling a standard DOS routine instead of
                by invoking standard BIOS routines.
 
  Default:      Off
