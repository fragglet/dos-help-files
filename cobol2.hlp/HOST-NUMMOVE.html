  Directive:    HOST-NUMMOVE
 
  Function:     Causes the compiler to switch off RTS error 163 checking
                during the execution of certain move statements.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬─HOST-NUMMOVE───────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOHOST-NUMMOVE
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Any
