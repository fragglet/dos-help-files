  Directive:    ACCEPTREFRESH
 
  Function:     Specifies whether the data areas associated with Screen
                Section data are updated from their corresponding Working-
                Storage Section items before an ACCEPT statement.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─┬────┬──ACCEPTREFRESH─────────────►◄
        └─/─┘ └─NO─┘
 
  Parameters:   None
 
  Default:      NOACCEPTREFRESH
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Initial
