Switch A1 (↑ RTS Switches)
 ◄Key► ◄Details►                             ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Switch:       A1
 
  Function:     Disables the display of trailing spaces by causing
                DISPLAY ... UPON CONSOLE statements to strip off any
                trailing spaces in the data item being displayed before
                outputting the data to the screen.
 
  Default:      On
