OLDREADINTO Details (↑ Choosing Run-time Behavior)
 ◄Key► ◄Summary►                             ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     When OLDREADINTO is specified, the IMPLICIT move from the file's
     record area to the data-item specified in the INTO phrase is
     executed even when the READ is not successful. If NOOLDREADINTO is
     specified, the MOVE only happens if the READ is successful.
