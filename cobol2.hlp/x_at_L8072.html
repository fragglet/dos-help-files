     This directive is reserved for use with add-on products supplied
     by Micro Focus. Do not change its setting unless you have an
     appropriate add-on product. This is not for use with OS/2.
 
     With NOCICSOPTIMIZE, the compiler inserts instructions whenever a
     BLL-cell is updated to ensure that the addressability of Linkage
     Section items corresponds to the current values in the BLL cells.
     With CICSOPTIMIZE, the compiler does not insert these instructions.
     You must use the SERVICE RELOAD instruction to ensure that the
     addressability is updated.
