────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Directive:    MAKESYN
 
  Function:     Makes one reserved word synonymous with another.
 
  Syntax:
 
     ►►─┬───┬─MAKESYN─"rsv-word-1"─=─"rsv-word-2"─►◄
        └─/─┘
 
  Parameters:   rsv-word-1           A reserved word.
                rsv-word-2           The reserved word whose meaning
                                     is to be changed.
 
  Default:      No reserved word synonyms are created.
 
  Phase:        Syntax check
 
  $SET:         Initial
