Animate Command
 
  Use the Animate command to highlight each statement as
  it is executed. Animation helps you understand the
  control flow of your program.  When animation is on,
  you can press CTRL+BREAK to stop execution.
 
  TIP: Control the speed of animation with the Animate
  Speed settings of the Run/Debug command in the Options
  menu.
