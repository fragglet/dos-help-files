Help Window Command
  Use the Help Window command to display help on the
  help system, Pascal language, and standard units.
  All the usual read-only editor commands (PgUp, PgDn,
  Search, Copy, etc.), plus F1 and the Right mouse button,
  work in this window.
 
    ■ Press F1 to activate a hyperlink.
 
    ■ Press TAB to move forward or SHIFT+TAB to move
      backward through the hyperlinks.
 
    ■ Press a lowercase letter to move to the next
      hyperlink starting with that letter. An uppercase
      letter moves to the previous hyperlink starting with
      that letter. (This is useful in the index.)
