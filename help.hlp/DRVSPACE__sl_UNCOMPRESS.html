DRVSPACE /UNCOMPRESS
◄Notes►  ◄Example►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
                            DRVSPACE /UNCOMPRESS
 
Uncompresses a drive that was compressed by using DriveSpace. When you
uncompress the last mounted drive, the /UNCOMPRESS switch also removes
DRVSPACE.BIN from memory. (DRVSPACE.BIN is the portion of MS-DOS that
provides access to compressed drives. It uses about 50K of memory.)
 
Syntax
 
    DRVSPACE /UNCOMPRESS drive:
 
Parameter
 
drive:
    Specifies the drive you want to uncompress.
 
Switch
 
/UNCOMPRESS
    Uncompresses the specified drive.
