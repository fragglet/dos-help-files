DRVSPACE /LASTDRIVE
 
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
                            DRVSPACE /LASTDRIVE
 
Modifes the LASTDRIVE setting in the <DRVSPACE.INI> file. The LASTDRIVE
setting specifies the highest drive letter available for use by DriveSpace.
 
Note:  For this switch to take effect, you must restart your computer.
 
Syntax
 
    DLBSPACE /LASTDRIVE=drive
 
Parameter
 
drive
 
    The drive letter that is the highest drive letter you want to be
    available to DriveSpace. (If another program uses one of the drive
    letters specified for DriveSpace, the highest drive letter available to
    DriveSpace will be higher than that specified by LASTDRIVE.)
