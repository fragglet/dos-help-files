Edit menu
Use to:
    ■ Delete (or "cut") text
    ■ Copy text
    ■ Move ("cut and paste") text
    ■ Reverse (or "undo") your last edit
    ■ Reverse (or "redo") your last undo
    ■ Erase text without adding it to the "Clipboard"
    ■ Create new SUB and FUNCTION procedures
