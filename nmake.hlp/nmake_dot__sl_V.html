     Syntax:  /V
 
     Causes all macros to be inherited when recursing. By default, only
     macros defined on the command line and environment-variable macros
     are inherited when NMAKE is called recursively. This option makes
     all macros available to the recursively called NMAKE session.
     See: ◄Calling NMAKE Recursively►
