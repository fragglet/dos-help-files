NMAKE Options
                                             ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
─────NMAKE──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     Options control the NMAKE session. Options are not case sensitive
     and can be preceded by either a slash (/) or a dash (-).
 
     You can specify some options in the makefile or in TOOLS.INI.
     Others can be made available in environment-variable macros.
     See: ◄!CMDSWITCHES Directive►
          ◄Environment-Variable Macros►
          ◄Options Macros►
          ◄The TOOLS.INI File►
