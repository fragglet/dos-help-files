     NMAKE Fatal Error U1021
 
     syntax error : !ELSE unexpected
 
     An !ELSE directive was found that was not preceded by an !IF
     directive, or the directive was placed in a syntactically
     incorrect place.
 
     The !IF preprocessing directives include !IF, !IFDEF, !IFNDEF,
     !ELSE IF, !ELSE IFDEF, and !ELSE IFNDEF.
