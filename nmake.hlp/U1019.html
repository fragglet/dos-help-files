     NMAKE Fatal Error U1019
 
     too many nested !IF blocks
 
     The limit on nesting of !IF directives was exceeded.
 
     The !IF preprocessing directives include !IF, !IFDEF, !IFNDEF,
     !ELSE IF, !ELSE IFDEF, and !ELSE IFNDEF.
