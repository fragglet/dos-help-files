     NMAKE Warning U4005
 
     ignoring rule 'rule' (extension not in .SUFFIXES)
 
     The given rule contained a suffix that is not specified in the
     .SUFFIXES list. NMAKE ignored the rule.
 
     This warning appears only when the /P option is used.
