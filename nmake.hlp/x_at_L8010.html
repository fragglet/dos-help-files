/P Option
                                             ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
─────NMAKE──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     Syntax:  /P
 
     Displays NMAKE information to standard output before running the
     NMAKE session. The information includes all macro definitions,
     inference rules, target descriptions, and the .SUFFIXES list. If
     /P is specified without a makefile or command-line target, NMAKE
     displays the information and does not issue an error.
 
     The /P and /D options are useful for debugging a makefile.
     See: ◄/D Option►
