     Syntax:  /S
 
     Suppresses the display of all executed commands.
 
     To suppress the display of commands in part of a makefile, use the
     @ command modifier or the .SILENT directive. To set or clear /S
     for part of a makefile, use the !CMDSWITCHES directive.
     See: ◄At-Sign (@) Modifier►
