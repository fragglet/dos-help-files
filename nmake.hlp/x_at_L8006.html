─────NMAKE──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     Syntax:  /C
 
     Suppresses the default NMAKE information display, including
     nonfatal NMAKE error or warning messages, time stamps, and the
     NMAKE copyright message. If both /C and /K are specified, /C
     suppresses the warnings issued by /K.
     See: ◄/K Option►
