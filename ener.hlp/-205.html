Translation momentarily stopped
■ Visual Basic has stopped its item-by-item translation of
  the program line at the highlighted item. The error is
  ordinarily to the left of the item.
 
■ Possible solutions:
 
  • Substitute one of the items suggested in the dialog
    box immediately to the left of the highlighted item.
 
  • Correct any misspelled keywords to the left of the
    highlighted item.
 
■ If you need additional help, move the cursor to a keyword
  and press F1 to open the Help window.
