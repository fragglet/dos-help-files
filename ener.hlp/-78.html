Expression too complex
■ Certain internal limitations of Visual Basic have been
  exceeded.
 
■ For example, during expression evaluation, strings that are
  not associated with variables are assigned temporary
  locations. A large number of such strings or a numeric
  expression with many complicated subexpressions can cause
  this error.
 
■ Possible solution:
 
  • Simplify expressions or assign strings to variables.
 
■ If you need additional help, move the cursor to a keyword
  and press F1 to open the Help window.
