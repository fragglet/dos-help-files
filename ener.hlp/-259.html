EXIT FOR not within FOR...NEXT
■ An EXIT FOR statement is used outside a FOR...NEXT
  statement.
 
■ Possible solution:
 
  • Delete the EXIT FOR statement or reposition it within a
    FOR...NEXT structure.
 
■ If you need additional help, move the cursor to a keyword
  and press F1 to open the Help window.
