Can't use separator bar as menu name
■ You tried to put a separator bar in a top-level
  menu title. Top-level menu titles must be text.
 
■ Possible solutions:
 
  • Remove the hyphen from the Caption text box and replace it
    with text.
 
  • Remove the check mark from the Separator check box.
 
■ If you need additional help, move the cursor to a keyword
  and press F1 to open the Help window.
