Identifier cannot end with %, &, !, #, $, or @
■ These suffixes are not allowed in type identifiers, SUB
  procedure names, or names appearing in COMMON statements.
 
■ Possible solution:
 
  • Verify that your type identifiers do not include the
    following suffixes:
         • % (percent)   • & (ampersand)  • ! (exclamation)
         • # (pound)     • $ (dollar)     • @ (at)
 
■ If you need additional help, move the cursor to a keyword
  and press F1 to open the Help window.
