LOOP without DO
■ The DO keyword that starts a DO...LOOP statement is missing
  or misspelled.
 
■ Possible solutions:
 
  • Insert the DO keyword at the beginning of the DO...LOOP
    structure or correct misspelling as necessary.
 
  • Check other control structures within the DO...LOOP block
    to verify that they are correctly matched.
 
■ If you need additional help, move the cursor to a keyword
  and press F1 to open the Help window.
