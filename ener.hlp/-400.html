Form already displayed: can't show form modally (ERR: 400)
■ You cannot use the SHOW method to display a modal form if
  the form is already visible.
 
■ Possible solutions:
 
  • Don't try to display the form as a modal form.
 
  • Unload or hide the form before trying to display it as a
    modal form.
 
■ If you need additional help, move the cursor to a keyword
  and press F1 to open the Help window.
