Menu item skipped a level
■ Menu levels must be contiguous. You can create up to four
  levels of submenus can be created. An item at level two,
  for instance, can open a submenu at level three, and an item
  at level three can open a submenu at level four.
 
■ Possible solution:
 
  • In the Menu Design window, make sure menu items are
    indented only one level past the previous level.
 
■ If you need additional help, move the cursor to a keyword
  and press F1 to open the Help window.
