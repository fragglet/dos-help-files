Statement illegal in TYPE block
■ The only statements allowed between the TYPE and END TYPE
  statements are REM and AS clauses.
 
■ Possible solution:
 
  • Remove the highlighted statement or insert a REM or AS
    clause if necessary.
 
■ If you need additional help, move the cursor to a keyword
  and press F1 to open the Help window.
