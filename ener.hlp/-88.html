ISAM - Database needs repair (ERR: 88)
■ An OPEN FOR ISAM statement attempted to open a file that
  needs repair.
 
■ Possible solution:
 
  • Use the ISAMREPR.EXE utility to recover (restore physical
    integrity to) the database.
 
■ If you need additional help, move the cursor to a keyword
  and press F1 to open the Help window.
