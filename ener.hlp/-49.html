Invalid in SUB, FUNCTION, or DEF FN
■ The statement is not allowed in a SUB or FUNCTION procedure
  or DEF FN function.
 
■ Possible solution:
 
  • Move the highlighted statement to the module level of a
    non-form module.
 
■ If you need additional help, move the cursor to a keyword
  and press F1 to open the Help window.
