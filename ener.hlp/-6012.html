Exit (Alt+F4)
■ Leaves the Form Designer
 
■ If you started the Form Designer from MS-DOS, you will be
  returned to the MS-DOS prompt. If you started the Form
  Designer by choosing New Form or View Form from within
  Visual Basic, you will be returned to the programming
  environment.
 
■ Note: If a new or changed form has not been saved, the
        Form Designer prompts you to confirm before exiting.
