Reached limit: cannot create any more controls for this form (345)
■ No more than 255 menu items or controls are allowed on each
  form.
 
■ Possible solution:
 
  • Redesign your application so that a second form can be
    created to contain some of the controls.
 
■ If you need additional help, move the cursor to a keyword
  and press F1 to open the Help window.
