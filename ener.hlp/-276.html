COMMON in Quick library too small
■ More COMMON variables are specified in the module than in
  the currently loaded Quick library.
 
■ Possible solutions:
 
  • Remove some of the variables specified in the module.
 
  • Recreate the Quick library to account for COMMON
    variables.
 
■ If you need additional help, move the cursor to a keyword
  and press F1 to open the Help window.
