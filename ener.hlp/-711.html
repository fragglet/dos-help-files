Separator may not be checked or disabled, or have shortcut key
■ Menu controls with their Separator box checked may not be:
  • Checked (Checked box is selected)
  • Disabled (Enabled box is not selected)
  • Assigned a shortcut key
 
■ Possible solutions:
 
  • Remove the check mark from the Separator box before:
    ♦ Selecting the Checked box.
    ♦ Removing the check mark from the Enabled box.
    ♦ Assigning a shortcut key.
 
  • Remove the check mark in the Checked box.
 
  • Select the Enabled box.
 
  • Reassign the shortcut key to "(none)".
 
■ If you need additional help, move the cursor to a keyword
  and press F1 to open the Help window.
