Statement cannot precede SUB/FUNCTION definition
■ The only statements allowed before a procedure definition
  are REM, DEFtype, EVENT ON, and EVENT OFF.
 
■ Possible solutions:
 
  • Move the highlighted statement to the module-level code.
 
  • Move the highlighted statement within the SUB or FUNCTION
    template.
 
■ If you need additional help, move the cursor to a keyword
  and press F1 to open the Help window.
