Division by zero (ERR: 11)
■ This error is caused by dividing by zero in an expression.
 
■ Possible solutions:
 
  • Check the variable values and intermediate calculations
    to identify the source of the miscalculation.
 
  • Incorporate error checking into your routine to prevent
    this error.
 
■ If you need additional help, move the cursor to a keyword
  and press F1 to open the Help window.
