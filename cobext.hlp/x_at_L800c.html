COBOL Subscript Bound Checking
                                             ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     Use the Subscript Bound Checking option to check the subscript or
     index value on each access of a table. This ensures that the
     subscript or index is within the limits implied by the OCCURS
     clause.
 
     Command-line directive: BOUND
