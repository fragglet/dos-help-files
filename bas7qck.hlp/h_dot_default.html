     How to Get Help
 
     ┌──┐              Press F1 to get help on
     │F1│                ■ The keyword, program object, or topic
     └──┘                  your cursor is on
                         ■ An open menu item or menu title
                         ■ An open dialog box
 
     Right mouse       Point with the mouse cursor and press the
     button            right mouse button.
 
     ┌─────┐   ┌──┐
     │Shift│ + │F1│    Press Shift+F1 to view this screen at any time.
     └─────┘   └──┘
 
Look for the Help display in either:
    ■ The Help window at the top of the screen
    ■ A dialog box that pops up in the center of the screen
 
In the Help window, hyperlinks help you find information related to
what is on the screen.
    ■ All BASIC keywords are hyperlinks.
    ■ Other hyperlinks can be identified because they are bounded
      by highlighted arrowheads.
    ■ Press Tab to move the cursor to a hyperlink.
 
  Tip: Press F6 or Shift+F6 to move the cursor into the Help window.
 
The QBX environment saves the 20 most recently used hyperlinks. To review
them, press Alt+F1.
 
  Tip: The Status bar at the bottom of the screen displays information about
       some of the most useful Help commands and keystrokes. If the command
       has angle brackets around it, you can choose it with a mouse.
