TIME$ = stringexpression$
 
    ■ stringexpression$   The time in one of the following forms:
                          hh          Sets the hour; minutes and seconds
                                      default to 00.
                          hh:mm       Sets the hour and minutes; seconds
                                      default to 00.
                          hh:mm:ss    Sets the hour, minutes, and seconds.
