──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
DATE$ = stringexpression$
 
    ■ stringexpression$   The date in one of the following forms:
                          mm-dd-yy, mm-dd-yyyy, mm/dd/yy, or mm/dd/yyyy
                          where:
                            mm (01-12)                Is the month.
                            dd (01-31)                Is the day.
                            yy or yyyy (1980-2099)    Is the year.
