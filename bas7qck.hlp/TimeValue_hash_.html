TimeValue# (time$)
 
    ■ time$    A time between 0:00:00 (12:00:00 AM) and 23:59:59
               (11:59:59 PM), inclusive. Time can be entered as "2:24PM"
               or "14:24".
 
Returns
    A serial number that represents the time of the argument.
