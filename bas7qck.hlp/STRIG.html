STRIG Statements
  ◄Syntax►  ◄Details►  ◄Example►                 ◄Contents►  ◄Index►  ◄Back►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
STRIG(n%) ON
STRIG(n%) OFF
STRIG(n%) STOP
 
    ■ n%               The trigger number (an integer expression):
                           n%    Trigger  Joystick
                           0     Lower    First
                           2     Lower    Second
                           4     Upper    First
                           6     Upper    Second
    ■ linenumber or    The first line of the event-handling routine to
      linelabel        branch to when joystick trigger n% is pressed.
 
    ■ STRIG(n%) ON      Enables joystick event trapping.
    ■ STRIG(n%) OFF     Disables joystick event trapping.
    ■ STRIG(n%) STOP    Suspends joystick event trapping.
 
    ■ The STRIG statements are not available for OS/2 protected mode.
