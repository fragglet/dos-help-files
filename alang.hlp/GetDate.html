  Include:   DOS.INC
 
  Syntax:    @GetDate
 
  Summary:   Gets the system date
 
  Arguments: None
 
  Returns:   AL     Day of week (0 = Sunday, 1 = Monday, ...)
             CX     Year (1980-2099)
             DH     Month (1-12)
             DL     Day (1-31)
 
  Modifies:  AX, CX, DX
 
  See also:  Int 21h Function 2Ah, @SetDate, @GetTime
