  Title:    Load Constant
 
  Syntax:   FLDconst
 
  See also: FLD
 
  Description:
 
     Pushes a constant onto the stack. The following constants can be
     loaded:
 
     Instruction     Constant
 
     FLD1            +1.0
 
     FLDZ            +0.0
 
     FLDPI           pi
 
     FLDL2E          Log (e)
                        2
 
     FLDL2T          Log (10)
                        2
 
     FLDLG2          Log  (2)
                        10
 
     FLDLN2          Log (2)
                        e
                                    -♦-
