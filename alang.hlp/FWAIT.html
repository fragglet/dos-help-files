FWAIT Instruction
 ◄Detail► ◄Example►                          ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Title:    Wait
 
  Syntax:   FWAIT
 
  See also: WAIT
 
  Description:
 
     Suspends execution of the processor until the coprocessor is
     finished executing. This is an alternate mnemonic for the
     processor WAIT instruction.
