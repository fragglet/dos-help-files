  Title:    Subtract                              Flags: O D I T S Z A P C
                                                         ═════════════════
  Syntax:   SUB dest,src                                 ±       ± ± ± ± ±
 
  See also: DEC, SBB, ADD
 
  Description:
 
     Subtracts the source operand from the destination operand and
     stores the result in the destination operand.
