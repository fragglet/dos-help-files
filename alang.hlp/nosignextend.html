  Syntax:   OPTION NOSIGNEXTEND
 
  See also: AND, OR, XOR
 
  Description:
 
     The NOSIGNEXTEND option disables the generation of the sign-
     extended form (83h) of the AND, OR, and XOR instructions. The NEC
     V25 and V35 microprocessors do not support this opcode.
