  Title:    One's-Complement Negation             Flags: O D I T S Z A P C
                                                         ═════════════════
  Syntax:   NOT dest                                          -none-
 
  See also: OR Operator, NEG, AND, OR, XOR, SHL, SHR
 
  Description:
 
     Toggles each bit of the operand by clearing set bits and setting
     cleared bits.
