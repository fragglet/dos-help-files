_GetPhysCoord Procedure
  ◄Summary► ◄Details► ◄Example►                                   ◄Back►
 
  Arguments
 
    x, y    Viewport coordinates to be translated
 
    xy      Record specifying physical screen coordinates corresponding
            to viewport coordinates x and y
 
  Description
 
    The _GetPhysCoord procedure translates the viewport coordinates
    (x,y) to physical coordinates and places the result in the xy
    record variable.
 
  _GrStatus Returns
 
    _GrNotInProperMode
