     Action
 
     Does not have any effect.
 
     Syntax
 
     CONTINUE
 
     Remarks
 
     The CONTINUE statement is a convenient place for a label,
     particularly as the terminal statement in a DO or DO WHILE loop.
 
     Example
 
           DIMENSION narray(10)
           DO 100, n = 1, 10
           narray(n) = 120
       100 CONTINUE
