────ANSI Conformance───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     Microsoft FORTRAN 5.1 conforms to the American National Standard
     Programming Language FORTRAN 77, as described in the American
     National Standards Institute (ANSI) X3.9-1978 and ISO 1539-1980
     (E) standards.
 
     Microsoft FORTRAN 5.1 also contains many extensions to the full
     ANSI standard language. These extensions appear in blue in the
     manuals. Online help does not differentiate between the standard
     language and extensions.
