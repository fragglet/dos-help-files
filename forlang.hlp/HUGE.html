─────HUGE Attribute─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     Allows a formal argument or allocatable array to exceed 64K (one
     data segment).
 
     Restrictions: Cannot apply to common block names or subprogram
     names
 
     See Also: ◄$LARGE►
