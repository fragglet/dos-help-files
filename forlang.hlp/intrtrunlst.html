─────Truncating and Rounding────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     AINT and DINT truncate their arguments. Any decimal portion of the
     argument is removed from the returned value. ANINT, DNINT, NINT,
     and IDNINT round their arguments to the closest integer value.
