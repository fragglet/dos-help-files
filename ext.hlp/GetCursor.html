─────PWB Extensions─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax: void GetCursor( COL far *px, LINE far *py );
 
  See:    FileLength, MoveCur
 
     GetCursor gets the current line and column positions of the cursor.
     The values are returned through the <px> and <py> pointers.
 
     Extension coordinates are always zero-based.
