CloseWnd
                                             ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
─────PWB Extensions─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  flagType CloseWnd( PWND pWnd );
 
  Returns: True if pWnd was closed, otherwise false.
 
  See:     NewWindow, Resize, SplitWnd, ◄Using Windows►
 
     CloseWnd removes the open window specified by <pWnd> from the
     screen.
 
     You cannot use CloseWnd to close a pop-up window created with
     PopUpBox.
