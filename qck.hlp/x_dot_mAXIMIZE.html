 Maximize Button
 
 ■ A Maximize button enlarges a window to full-screen size. It is located in
   the upper-right corner of the window:

   See: ◄MaxButton Property►
 
 ■ The top and bottom lines of the screen are reserved for the menu bar and
   status bar and cannot be covered by a window.
   See: ◄Menu Definitions►  ◄Status Bar Definition►
 
 ■ When a window is maximized, the Maximize button changes to the Restore
   button. Only the Restore, Minimize, and Close options are enabled on the
   Control menu.
