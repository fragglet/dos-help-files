CDECL Keyword
  ◄Summary►             ◄Example►                ◄Contents►  ◄Index►  ◄Back►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 In a DECLARE statement that declares a non-Basic procedure, CDECL indicates
 that the declared procedure uses the C-language argument order. Visual Basic
 passes the arguments in the argument list from right to left, rather than
 from left to right, as is the usual Basic convention.
