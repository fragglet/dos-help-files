 Determines a scroll bar position's maximum (Max) or minimum (Min) value.
 
    [form.]{hscrollbar. | vscrollbar.}Max[ = limit%]
    [form.]{hscrollbar. | vscrollbar.}Min[ = limit%]
 
    ■ limit%       Valid settings are:
 
                   Value               Description
                   ═════════════════   ═════════════════════════════════════
                   -32,768 to 32,767   Valid change setting
                   32,767              (Default) Max
                   0                   (Default) Min
 
 Applies To
    ◄Horizontal Scroll Bar Control►    ◄Vertical Scroll Bar Control►
