 Sets or returns the alignment of text in a label control.
 
    [form.]label.Alignment[ = setting%]
 
    ■ setting%     Valid settings are:
 
                   Value     Justification
                   ═════     ═══════════════════════════════════════════════
                   0         (Default) Left
                   1         Right
                   2         Center
