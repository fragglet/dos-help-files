 Opening and Closing the Immediate Window
 
 ■ To open the Immediate window:
   • From the Window menu, choose Immediate Window
 
 ■ To close the Immediate window:
   • Double-click the window's Control-menu box or press Ctrl+F4 when the
     window is active. See: ◄Control-Menu Box Definition►
