StringRelease Routine
  ◄Summary►  ◄Details►  ◄Example►                ◄Contents►  ◄Index►  ◄Back►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 Used by a non-Basic routine to deallocate variable-length strings that have
 been transferred to Visual Basic's string space from the non-Basic routine.
 
    StringRelease(string-descriptor%);
 
    ■ string-descriptor%     Near address of a string descriptor within a
                             non-Basic routine
