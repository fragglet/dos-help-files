 Visual Basic Reserved Words
 
 ■ Reserved words are all terms that are recognized by the Visual Basic
   interpreter, including:
 
         • Keywords     • Functions    • Statements   • Operators
 
 ■ Reserved words may not be used as labels, or as names for variables or
   procedures.
 
 ■ The following words are reserved in Visual Basic:
 
   ABS                 ACCESS              ADDITEM             ALIAS
   ALL*                AND                 ANY                 APPEND
   AS                  ASC                 ATN
