 Opening a COM port
 
 ■ The following OPEN statement is generalized enough to be accepted by most
   configurations:
 
         OPEN "COM1: 300,N,8,1,BIN,CD0,CS0,DS0,OP0,RS,TB2048,RB2048" AS #1
 
 ■ If you still receive an error, you may have a hardware problem.
