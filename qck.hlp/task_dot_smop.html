 Sound/Music Operations
 
 ■ The following keywords are used in Visual Basic to perform sound and music
   operations:
 
   ◄BEEP Statement►                    Generates beep sound from speaker
   ◄ON PLAY Statement►                 Branches to routine on PLAY event
   ◄PLAY Function►                     Returns number of notes in music queue
   ◄PLAY Statement (Music)►            Plays musical notes
   ◄PLAY Statement (Event Trapping)►   Enables play-event trapping
   ◄SOUND Statement►                   Generates sounds from speaker
