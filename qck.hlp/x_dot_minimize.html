 Minimize Button
 
 ■ A Minimize button shrinks a window to an icon. It is located in the upper-
   right corner of the window:

 ■ The active window can be minimized by:
   • Clicking the Minimize button
   • Choosing Minimize from the Control menu
   • Pressing Ctrl+F9
