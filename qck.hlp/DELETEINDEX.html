DELETEINDEX Statement
  ◄Summary►             ◄Example►                ◄Contents►  ◄Index►  ◄Back►

                 ISAM available in Professional Edition only
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 Removes an index from an ISAM database. Requires PROISAM.EXE or
 PROISAMD.EXE in the programming environment (VBDOS.EXE).
 
    DELETEINDEX [#]filenumber%, indexname$
 
    ■ filenumber%    Number used in the OPEN statement to open the table;
                     integer expression with a value between 1 and 255,
                     inclusive
 
    ■ indexname$     Name of existing ISAM index (same name used in
                     CREATEINDEX statement)
