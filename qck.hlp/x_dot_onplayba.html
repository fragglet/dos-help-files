ON PLAY Statement
  ◄Summary►  ◄Details►  ◄Example►                ◄Contents►  ◄Index►  ◄Back►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 Branches to the specified routine when there are fewer than queuemin%
 notes in the background-music queue; can be used with object-specific
 events.
 
    ON PLAY(queuemin%) GOSUB {linenumber | linelabel}
 
    ■ queuemin%       Integer expression that is the minimum number of
                      notes to maintain in the background music queue
                      between 1 and 32, inclusive
 
    ■ linenumber      First line of an event-handling routine to execute
      linelabel       when the number of notes in the music queue is less
                      than queuemin%
