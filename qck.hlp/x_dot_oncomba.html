 Branches to the specified routine when characters are received at a
 communications port; can be used with object-specific events.
 
    ON COM(n%) GOSUB {linenumber | linelabel}
 
    ■ n%              Number of a COM (serial) port (1 or 2)
 
    ■ linenumber      First line of an event-handling routine to execute
      linelabel       when characters are received at a communications
                      port
