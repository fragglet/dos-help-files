 TO Keyword
 
 ■ The TO keyword specifies valid ranges for the following Visual Basic
   statements:
 
         Statement           Range
         ═══════════════     ═══════════════════════════════════════════════
         ◄DIM►               Lower and upper bounds
         ◄FOR...NEXT►        Loop counter
         ◄LOCK...UNLOCK►     Records to lock or unlock
         ◄REDIM►             Lower and upper bounds
         ◄SELECT CASE►       CASE clause
