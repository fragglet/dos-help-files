 SIGNAL Statements
 
 ■ The SIGNAL and ON SIGNAL statements are not supported in Visual Basic for
   MS-DOS. To maintain compatibility with Microsoft QuickBasic, the SIGNAL
   keyword is retained as a reserved word.
