 Submenu
 
 ■ A submenu is the menu that opens when you choose a menu item(or command)
   from a higher-level menu. Submenu items are indented in the Menu Design
   window to indicate their hierarchical position or level.
   See: ◄Menu Design Window►
 
 ■ You can create up to five levels of submenus in Visual Basic. However, in
   a complex menu system, it's a good idea to use dialog boxes to simplify
   tasks. See: ◄Creating a Dialog Box►
