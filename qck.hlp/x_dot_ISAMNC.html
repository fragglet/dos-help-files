 ISAM Naming Conventions
 
 ■ ISAM names use the following naming conventions:
   • Comprised of alphanumeric characters (A-Z, a-z, 0-9) only
   • Cannot include any special characters
   • Can be no more than 30 characters in length
   • Must begin with an alphabetic character
