 Generates a sound through your computer's speaker.
 
    SOUND frequency, duration
 
    ■ frequency    Frequency of the sound in hertz; a value in the range
                   37 to 32,767, inclusive
 
    ■ duration     Specifies number of system clock ticks the sound lasts; a
                   value in the range 0 to 65,535, inclusive; there are 18.2
                   clock ticks per second
