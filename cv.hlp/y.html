     Command: Delete Watch Expression (Y)
 
     Syntax:  Y {number|*}
 
     Summary: Removes a watch expression from the watch window.
 
     Arguments:
 
     <number>   Deletes the watch expression with this number.
 
     *          Deletes all watch expressions.
