     CodeView allows two types of single-stepping through a program:
     stepping over functions or ◄tracing into functions►.
 
     When you step over a function, the entire function is executed
     in a single step. Watch variables and registers are updated to
     reflect their values after the function return.
 
     To step over a function:
 
        ■ Press F10
 
        ■ Click <F10=Step> in the status line with the left mouse
          button
       OR
        ■ Enter the ◄Program Step (P)► command in the ◄command window►
