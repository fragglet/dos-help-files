     The Index command displays an alphabetic index of help topics.
 
       1. Type one of the letters shown and press ENTER to see a list
          of entries starting with that letter.
 
       2. Use the scroll bars and arrow keys to move through the list.
 
       3. Press F1 for help on the topic the cursor is on.
