Set Watch Expressions
                                             ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     "Watch expressions" are program variables (or ◄expressions►
     involving program variables) that you watch for changes while you
     ◄execute a program►. You can display watch expressions in the
     ◄watch window►.
 
     To add a watch expression to the watch window:
 
        ■ Choose the ◄Add Watch► command from the Watch menu
       OR
        ■ Enter the ◄Add Watch Expression (W?)► command in the
          ◄command window►
 
     To remove a watch expression from the watch window:
 
        ■ Choose the ◄Delete Watch► command from the Watch menu
 
        ■ Enter the ◄Delete Watch Expression (Y)► command in the
          command window
       OR
        ■ Move the cursor to the watch expression in the ◄watch window►
          and press CTRL+Y
