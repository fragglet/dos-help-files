Reusing Commands

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     To reuse a command:
 
       1. Choose a previously entered command. Press TAB to move to
          the next command, or press SHIFT+TAB to move to the
          previous command, or press the arrow keys to move through
          command and output lines.
 
       2. Edit the command if desired.
 
       3. Press ENTER. The command is copied to the end of the buffer
          and executed.
 
       4. Press CTRL+HOME to go to line 1 of the buffer.
 
     See: ◄Command Window►
