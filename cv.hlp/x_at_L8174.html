Copy and Paste Text
                                             ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     CodeView allows you to copy a line of text from one window and
     paste this text into any writable window (the ◄memory►, ◄local►,
     ◄watch►, ◄register►, or ◄command► window).
 
     To copy and paste text:
 
       1. ◄Select text► with the keyboard or mouse
 
       2. Choose the ◄Copy► command from the ◄Edit menu► or press
          CTRL+INS
 
       3. Move the cursor to the window where you want to paste the
          text by
 
             ■ Pressing F6 to move the cursor to the next open window
               on the screen
            OR
             ■ Choosing a command from the ◄View menu► to open a new
               window
 
       4. Position the cursor at the location where you want to insert
          the text
 
       5. Choose the ◄Paste► command from the ◄Edit menu► or press
          SHIFT+INS
