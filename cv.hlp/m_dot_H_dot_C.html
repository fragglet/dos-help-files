     Shortcut key: SHIFT+F1
 
     The Contents command displays a table of contents for CodeView
     debugger help.
 
       1. Use the scroll bars and arrow keys to move through the list.
 
       2. To see help on any topic, place the cursor on the topic and
          press F1.
