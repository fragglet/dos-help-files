     Shortcut key: F1
 
     Use the Topic command for help on a specific topic. Place the
     cursor anywhere on the topic you want to look up, or on the space
     following it. The topic may be in your program or in a CodeView
     help file.
 
     CodeView then displays the topic in the menu:
 
          Topic: xxxxxxxxxxx  F1
                     ▲
                     └───── First 11 letters of topic to look up
 
     TIP: Press F1 anywhere in the CodeView environment to search for
          help on the topic the cursor is on.
 
     See: ◄Using Help►
