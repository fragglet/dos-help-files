     Command: Restart (L)
 
     Syntax:  L argument[argument]...
 
     Summary: Restarts execution at the beginning of a program.
 
     Argument:
 
     <argument>   New arguments to the program, separated by spaces.
 
     NOTE: This command is not available in CVW.
