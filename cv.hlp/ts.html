     Command: Trace Speed (Ts, Tm, Tf)
 
     Syntax:  T{s|m|f}
 
     Argument:
 
     {s|m|f}   The lowercase character that specifies the trace speed:
 
               Letter     Speed
 
               s          Slow (1/2 second between steps)
               m          Medium (1/4 second between steps; default)
               f          Fast (no wait between steps)
