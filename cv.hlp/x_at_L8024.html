Disable Mouse (/M)
                                             ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     The /M option tells CodeView to ignore a mouse installed on your
     system. Use this option if
 
        ■ You are debugging a program that supports the mouse and your
          mouse is not a Microsoft mouse. The program's use of the
          mouse and CodeView's use of the mouse may be in conflict.
 
        ■ You have screen flipping turned on and you debug in OS/2
          running in the DOS session.
          See: ◄Screen Swapping and Flipping►
 
        ■ You are setting breakpoints in code that is responsive to
          mouse movements on the Windows application screen.
