     Command: Trace (T)
 
     Syntax:  T [count]
 
     Summary: Executes the current line or instruction, tracing
              into functions or assembly-language CALL instructions.
              The Program Step (P) command steps over functions and CALL
              instructions.
 
     Argument:
 
     <count>   Executes the command <count> times before stopping.
 
     See: ◄Program Step (P) Command-Window Command►
          ◄Use with Thread Command►
