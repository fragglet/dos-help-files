Use Black-and-White Display (/B)
                                             ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     The /B option forces CodeView to display in two colors even if you
     have a color adapter (CGA, EGA, or compatible). This option makes
     the CodeView display easier to read on monochrome displays.
 
     See: ◄Use 8514 and VGA Displays (/8)►
