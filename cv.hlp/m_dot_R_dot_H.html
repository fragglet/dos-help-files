Run Menu: History On Command
                                             ◄Up► ◄Contents► ◄Index► ◄Back►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     The History On command tells the debugger to create or use a
     record of a debugging session. This record is known as a "debug
     history."
 
     You can turn debug history on
 
        ■ At the beginning of a debugging session
       OR
        ■ Immediately after executing a Replay command from the Run
          menu
 
     See: ◄Debug History►
          ◄Run Menu: Restart Command►
          ◄Run Menu: Replay Command►
          ◄Run Menu: Undo Command►
          ◄Create New History►
          ◄Use Existing History►
 
     NOTE: The History On command is not available in CVW.
