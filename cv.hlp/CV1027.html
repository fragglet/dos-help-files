Invalid radix (use 8, 10, or 16) (CV1027)
 
     The Radix (N) command takes three radixes: 8 (octal), 10
     (decimal), and 16 (hexadecimal). Other radixes are not permitted.
     The new radix is always entered as a decimal number, regardless of
     the current radix.
