     Command: Go (G)
 
     Syntax:  G [breakaddress]
 
     Summary: Executes the program, beginning at the current address.
 
     Argument:
 
     <breakaddress>   An expression giving the address at which the
                      execution breaks. This expression can have any
                      of the following forms:
 
                           [module!]address
                           [module!]label
                           [module!].linenumber
