     Command: Unassemble (U)
 
     Syntax:  U [viewaddress]
 
     Argument:
 
     <viewaddress>   Shows unassembled instructions, beginning at this
                     address. The address may have any of the
                     following forms:
 
                          [module!]address
                          [module!]label
                          [module!].linenumber
