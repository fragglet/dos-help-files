     You cannot use the Restart (L) command while you are recording
     debug history.
     See: ◄Debug History►
 
     If the application redefines interrupts, the Restart command may
     not work correctly.
 
     In multiprocess programs, Restart does not automatically kill
     child processes or their controlling CodeView processes.
 
     See: ◄Debug Multiple Processes (/O)►
