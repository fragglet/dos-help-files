     Command: Assemble (A)
 
     Syntax:  A [address]
 
     Summary: Assembles 8086-family instructions and places them
              at a designated address in memory.
 
     Argument:
 
     <address>   Begins assembly at this address. If not given,
                 assembly begins at the current assembly address.
