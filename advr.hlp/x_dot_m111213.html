 Screen Modes 11, 12, and 13
 
 ■ Applies to VGA or MCGA adapters
 
 ■ Mode 11:
   • 640 x 480 graphics
   • 80 x 30 or 80 x 60 text format, character box size of 8 x 16 or 8 x 8
   • Up to 256K colors assigned to two color attributes
 
 ■ Mode 12 (VGA adapters only):
   • 640 x 480 graphics
   • 80 x 30 or 80 x 60 text format, character box size of 8 x 16 or 8 x 8
   • Up to 256K colors assigned to 16 color attributes
 
 ■ Mode 13:
   • 320 x 200 graphics
   • 40 x 25 text format, character box size of 8 x 8
   • Up to 256K colors assigned to up to 256 color attributes
