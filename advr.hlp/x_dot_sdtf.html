 Sample Date and Time Formats
 
 ■ The following are examples of date and time formats:
 
         FORMAT$ Expression     Display
         ══════════════════     ════════════════════════════════════════════
         m/d/yy                 12/7/58
         d-mmmm-yy              7-December-58
         d-mmmm                 7-December
         mmmm-yy                December-58
         h:mm AM/PM             8:50 PM
         h:mm:ss AM/PM          8:50:35 PM
         h:mm                   20:50
         h:mm:ss                20:50:35
         m/d/yy h:mm            12/7/58 20:50
