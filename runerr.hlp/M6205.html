     Math error M6205
 
     'function' : _TLOSS error
 
     A total loss of significance (precision) occurred.
 
     This error may be caused by giving a very large number as the
     operand of sin, cos, or tan because the operand must be reduced
     to a number between 0 and 2*pi.
