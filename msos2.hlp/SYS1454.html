SYS1454
 
Explanation
 
Supported languages are:
 
      BE (Belgium)              NO (Norway)
      CF (Canada Fr.)           PO (Portugal)
      DK (Denmark)              SF (Switzerland, Fr.
      GR (Germany)              SG (Switzerland Ger.)
      FR (France)               SP (Spain)
      IT (Italy)                SU (Finland)
      LA (Latin America)        SV (Sweden)
      NL (Netherlands)          UK (United Kingdom)
 
 
Action
 
Enter the correct keyboard code and retry the command.
