SYS1281
 
Explanation
 
Either track 0 is defective, or the diskette and drive type are
incompatible.
 
Action
 
Make sure the diskette and drive type are compatible and take appropriate
Action. If track 0 is defective, discard the diskette or repartition the
fixed disk.
