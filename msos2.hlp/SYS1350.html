SYS1350
 
Explanation
 
A subdirectory was found that did not properly contain a . entry. This
usually happens when MS OS/2 is not given a chance to update the disk
properly. During the updating process, the system may have shut down, or you
may have reloaded the system before MS OS/2 finished the update.
 
Action
disk.
