SYS1472
SYS1472
 
Explanation
 
The fixed disk or diskette may be full. (Swapping requires a minimum of
512KB free space.) Or, the diskette is write-protected.
 
Action
 
Do one of the following:
 
1. Make additional space available on the
   fixed disk or diskette.
2. Insert a formatted diskette that is not
   write-protected.
3. Change the SWAPPATH= statement in CONFIG.SYS
   to specify a different drive.
 
Then restart the system.
