#define INCL_WINHEAP
 
 
SETMEMBACKPTR(npb)
 
The SETMEMBACKPTR macro creates a back pointer from the near pointer
returned by the WinAllocMem and WinReallocMem functions.
 
Parameter  Description
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
npb        Specifies the near pointer.
 
See Also
 
WinAllocMem, WinReallocMem
