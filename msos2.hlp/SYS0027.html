SYS0027
SYS0027
 
Explanation
 
The disk may be damaged, unformatted, or not compatible with MS OS/2.
 
Action
 
1. Make sure the diskette is properly inserted.
 
2. Check that the disk is not damaged.
 
3. Format the disk for MS OS/2.
 
Then retry the command.
