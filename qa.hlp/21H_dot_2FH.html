  Description:  Get DTA Address
 
  Obtains the current address of the disk transfer area (DTA) for FCB
  read/write operations.
 
  Input             Output
  ══════            ══════════════════════════════════════════
  AH=2FH            ES:BX=segment:offset of disk transfer area
