.LALL
   ◄Directive Summary►  ◄Summary►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  .LALL
 
  Enables listing of all source statements in a macro expansion,
  including normal comments, but excluding macro comments.
 
   See Also:  .SALL, .XALL, .LIST, .XLIST
