FINIT/FNINIT
   ◄Summary►  ◄Timings►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
Initialize Coprocessor
 
Syntax: F[N]INIT
 
Initializes the coprocessor and resets all the registers and flags to
their default values. The instruction has wait and no-wait versions. On
the 80387, the condition codes of the status word are cleared. On the
8087 and 80287, they are unchanged.
