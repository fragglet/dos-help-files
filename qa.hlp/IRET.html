Interrupt Return                                 Flags:  O D I T S Z A P C
Syntax:  IRET                                            ═════════════════
                                                         ± ± ± ± ± ± ± ± ±
 
 
  Returns control from an interrupt procedure to the interrupted code.
  The IRET instruction pops IP, CS, and the flags (in that order) and
  resumes execution.
