.186
   ◄Directive Summary►  ◄Summary►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:    .186
 
  Enables assembly of instructions for the 8086 processor, the 8087
  coprocessor, and the 80186 processor, and disables instructions
  specific to the 80286 and 80287 processors.
 
  See Also:  .286, .287, .8086, .8087
