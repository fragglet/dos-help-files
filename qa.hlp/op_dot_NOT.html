NOT Operator
   ◄Operator Summary►  ◄Summary►  ◄NOT Instruction►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  NOT expression
 
  Returns expression with all bits reversed (bitwise complement).
 
  expression   Must evaluate to a constant value.
 
   See Also:  AND, OR, XOR, MASK
