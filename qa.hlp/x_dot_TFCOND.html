.TFCOND
   ◄Directive Summary►  ◄Summary►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  .TFCOND
 
  Toggles the state of the assembler between .LFCOND and .SFCOND; these
  two directives determine whether or not false conditional blocks are
  included in assembler listings.
 
   See Also:  .LFCOND, .SFCOND, .LIST, .XLIST
