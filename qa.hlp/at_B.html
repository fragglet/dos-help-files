@B
   ◄Operator Summary►  ◄Summary►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax: @@:
          .
          .
          .
               JMP  @B
 
 
  @B equates to the last @@: code label, searching backward in the file.
  You can use @B as the target of any jump or conditional jump instruction.
 
  See Also:  @B, @@
