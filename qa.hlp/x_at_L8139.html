Int 16H Function 03H
   ◄Summary►  ◄Notes►  ◄Back►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
    Subfunctions 00H-04H are available on the PCjr but are not supported by
    the PC or PC/XT ROM BIOS. Subfunction 05H is available on PC/ATs with ROM
    BIOS's dated 11/15/85 and later, and on the PS/2.
 
    On the PC/AT and PS/2, the value in BH controls the amount of delay
    before the first repeat key is generated, and is a multiple of 250
    milliseconds:
    Value            Delay (msec.)
    ═════            ═════════════
    00H              250
    01H              500
    02H              750
    03H              1000
 
    On the PC/AT and PS/2, the value for the repeat rate in characters per
