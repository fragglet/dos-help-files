.XLIST
   ◄Directive Summary►  ◄Summary►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  .XLIST
 
  Suppresses program listing. Used in conjunction with .LIST to prevent
  a particular section of a source file from being listed.
 
   See Also:  .LIST, .LFCOND, .SFCOND, .TFCOND
