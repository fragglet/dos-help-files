AND Operator
   ◄Operator Summary►  ◄Summary►  ◄AND Instruction►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  expression1 AND expression2
 
  Returns result of a bitwise Boolean AND done on expression1 and
  expression2.
 
  expression1   Must evaluate to a constant value.
 
  expression2   Must evaluate to a constant value.
 
   See Also:  NOT, OR, XOR
