XOR Operator
   ◄Operator Summary►  ◄Summary►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  expression1 XOR expression2
 
  Returns result of bitwise exclusive Boolean OR performed on
  expression1 and expression2.
 
  expression1   Must evaluate to a constant value.
 
  expression2   Must evaluate to a constant value.
 
   See Also:  NOT, AND, OR
