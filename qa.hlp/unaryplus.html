+ (Plus, Unary)
   ◄Operator Summary►  ◄Summary►  ◄Binary Plus►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  +expression
 
  Plus (unary). Designates a positive number. Has higher level of
  precedence than the binary (addition) plus.
 
  expression   An integer constant.
 
   See Also:  minus (-)
