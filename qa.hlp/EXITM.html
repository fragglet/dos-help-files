EXITM
   ◄Directive Summary►  ◄Summary►  ◄Example►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  EXITM
 
  Terminates expansion of the current macro or repeat block and begins
  assembly of the next statement outside the block.  If encountered in a
  nested macro block, the assembler returns to expanding the outer block.
 
  See Also:  IRP, IRPC, REPT, MACRO, ENDM
