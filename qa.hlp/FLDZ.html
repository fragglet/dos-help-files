Load Constant
 
Syntax:  FLDconst
 
Pushes a constant onto the stack. The following constants
can be loaded:
 
Instruction     Constant Loaded
FLD1            +1.0
FLDZ            +0.0
FLDPI           π
FLDL2E          Log(sub2)(e)
FLDL2T          Log(sub2)(10)
FLDLG2          Log(sub10)(2)
FLDLN2          Log(sub e)(2)
