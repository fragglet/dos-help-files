MOD
   ◄Operator Summary►  ◄Summary►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  expression1 MOD expression2
 
  Returns the remainder (modulus) of dividing expression1 by expression2.
 
  expression1   Must be an integer constant.
 
  expression2   Must be an integer constant.
 
   See Also:  slash (/)
