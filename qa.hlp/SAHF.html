Store AH into Flags                              Flags:  O D I T S Z A P C
                                                         ═════════════════
Syntax:  SAHF                                                    ± ± ± ± ±
 
 
  Transfers AH into bits 0 to 7 of the flags register. This includes
  the carry, parity, auxiliary carry, zero, and sign flags, but not the
  trap, interrupt, direction, or overflow flags.
