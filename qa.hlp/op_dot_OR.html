OR Operator
   ◄Operator Summary►  ◄Summary►  ◄OR Instruction►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  expression1 OR expression2
 
  Returns result of bitwise inclusive Boolean OR performed on
  expression1 and expression2.
 
  expression1   Must evaluate to a constant value.
 
  expression2   Must evaluate to a constant value.
 
   See Also:  NOT, AND, XOR
