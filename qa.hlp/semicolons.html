;; (Macro Comment Delimeter)
   ◄Operator Summary►  ◄Summary►  ◄Example►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  ;;text
 
  Treats text to end of line as a macro comment. The comment appears in
  the macro definition, but not in statements generated by macro
  expansion.
 
   See Also:  COMMENT, semicolon (;)
