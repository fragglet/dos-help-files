.SFCOND
   ◄Directive Summary►  ◄Summary►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  .SFCOND
 
  Suppresses listing of statements in conditional blocks whose condition
  evaluates to false (0) and were therefore not assembled. This is the
  default behavior.
 
   See Also:  .LFCOND, .TFCOND, .LIST, .XLIST
