* (Multiplication)
   ◄Operator Summary►  ◄Summary►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  expression1 * expression2
 
  Multiplication operator. Returns product of two expressions.
 
  expression1   Must be an integer constant.
 
  expression2   Must be an integer constant.
 
   See Also:  slash (/)
