──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  @CodeSize
 
  Predefined equate giving a value of 0 for tiny, small, and compact
  models, or 1 for medium, large, and huge models. The .MODEL directive
  must have previously been given.
 
  See Also:  @DataSize, .MODEL
