@DataSize
   ◄Directive Summary►  ◄Summary►  ◄Example►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  @DataSize
 
  Predefined equate giving a value of 0 for tiny, small, and medium
  models, or 1 for compact, large, and huge models. The .MODEL directive
  must have previously been given.
 
  See Also:  @CodeSize, .MODEL
