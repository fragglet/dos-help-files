FXTRACT
   ◄Summary►  ◄Timings►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
Extract Exponent and Significand
 
Syntax:  FXTRACT
 
Extracts the exponent and significand fields of ST. The exponent
replaces the value in ST, and then the significand is pushed onto the
stack.
