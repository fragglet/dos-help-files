──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  -expression
 
  Unary minus. Reverses sign of expression. Has higher level of
  precedence than the binary (subtraction) minus.
 
  expression   An integer constant.
 
   See Also:  plus (+)
