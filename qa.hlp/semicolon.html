; (Comment Delimeter)
   ◄Operator Summary►  ◄Summary►
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax:  ;text
 
  Treats text to end of line as a comment. The comment must follow all
  other fields in the statement.
 
   See Also:  COMMENT, semicolons (;;)
