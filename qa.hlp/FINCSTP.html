FINCSTP
   ◄Summary►  ◄Timings►
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
Increment Stack Pointer
 
Syntax:  FINCSTP
 
Increments the stack top pointer in the status word. No tags or
registers are changed and no data are transferred. If the stack pointer
is 7, then FINCSTP changes it to 0.
