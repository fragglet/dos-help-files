  Description:  Set Keyclick      [PC]
 
  Turns the keyboard click on or off.
 
  Input                                        Output
  ═════════════════════════════════            ═══════
  AH=04H                                       Nothing
  AL=subfunction
     00H to turn off keyboard click
     01H to turn on keyboard click
