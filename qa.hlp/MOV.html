Move Data
                                                 Flags:  O D I T S Z A P C
Syntax:  MOV dest,src                                    ═════════════════
 
 
  Copies the value in the source operand to the destination operand. If
  the destination operand is SS, then interrupts are disabled until
  the next instruction is executed (except on early versions of the 8088
  and 8086).
