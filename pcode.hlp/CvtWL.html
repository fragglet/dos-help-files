──P-Code Instructions───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
  Syntax
 
  CvtWL
 
  /* Convert word to long. */
 
  Description
 
  Pops the top word off the stack and signed extends it to a long value.
  The long value is then pushed onto the stack.
 
  Pseudocode equivalent:
 
  PushL((long) PopW());
