     Compiler warning (level 1) C4040
 
     memory attribute on 'identifier' ignored
 
     The _near, _far, _huge, or _based keyword has no effect in the
     declaration of the given identifier and is ignored.
 
     One cause of this warning is a huge array that is not declared
     globally. Declare huge arrays outside of main.
