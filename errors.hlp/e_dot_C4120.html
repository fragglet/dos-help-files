     Compiler warning (level 1) C4120
 
     _based/unbased mismatch
 
     The expression contains a conversion between a _based pointer and
     another pointer that is unbased. Some information may have been
     truncated.
 
     This warning commonly occurs when a _based pointer is passed to a
     function that accepts a near or far pointer.
