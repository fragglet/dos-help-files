     Command-line error D2008
 
     limit of  'option' exceeded at 'string'
 
     The limit of the /D, /I, or /U option was exceeded.
 
     Reduce the number of identifiers you define or undefine, or
     the number of directories to search for include files, to less
     than 30 each.
