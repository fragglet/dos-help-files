     LINK warning L4019
 
     /TINY disables /INCREMENTAL
 
     A .COM file always requires a full link and cannot be
     incrementally linked.
 
     The /TINY and /INCREMENTAL options are incompatible. When they are
     used together, the linker ignores /INCREMENTAL.
