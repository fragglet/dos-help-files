     LINK error L2003
 
     intersegment self-relative fix-up at 'number' in segment
     'segment'
 
     The program issued a near call or jump to a label in a different
     segment.
 
     This error most often occurs when specifically declaring an
     external procedure to be near that should be declared as far.
