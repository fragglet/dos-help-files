     Fatal error C1039
 
     unrecoverable heap overflow in Pass 3
 
     The post-optimizer compiler pass overflowed the heap and could not
     continue.
 
     One of the following may be a solution:
 
        ■ Break up the function containing the line that caused the
          error.
 
        ■ Recompile with the /Od option, removing optimization.
 
        ■ Remove other programs or drivers running in the system which
          could be consuming significant amounts of memory.
 
        ■ Compile without using NMAKE.
