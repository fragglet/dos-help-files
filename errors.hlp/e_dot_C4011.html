     Compiler warning (level 1) C4011
 
     identifier truncated to 'identifier'
 
     Only the first 31 characters of an identifier are significant. The
     characters after the limit were truncated.
 
     This may mean that two identifiers that are different before
     truncation may have the same identifier name after truncation.
