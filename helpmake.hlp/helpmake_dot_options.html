─────HELPMAKE Options───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     HELPMAKE accepts a number of command-line options. You can specify
     options in uppercase or lowercase letters and precede them with a
     forward slash (/). For example, /L and /l are equivalent.
 
     The E/D column below indicates whether the option is applicable to
     encoding (E), decoding (D), both (ED), or neither (*):
 
     Option         E/D     Action
 
     /Ac            E       ◄Specifies control character►
     /C             E       ◄Sets case sensitivity on►
     /D[c]          D       ◄Specifies decoding►
     /E[n]          E       ◄Specifies encoding►
     /H[ELP]        *       ◄Displays help on HELPMAKE►
     /Kfilename     E       ◄Specifies word-separator characters►
     /L             E       ◄Locks the output file►
     /Ooutfile      ED      ◄Specifies the output file►
     /Sn            E       ◄Specifies the type of input file►
     /T             ED      ◄Translates commands►
     /V[n]          ED      ◄Specifies diagnostic output►
     /Wn            E       ◄Specifies the width of help text►
     /?             *       ◄Displays summary of HELPMAKE►
