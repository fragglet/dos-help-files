─────Minimally Formatted ASCII: Example─────────────────────────────────────
 
     In the following example, three context strings are defined for a
     topic. No formatting flags or links are defined since minimally
     formatted ASCII does not support them.
 
     >>ContextA
     >>ContextB
     >>ContextC
     This topic text is associated with three context strings:
     ContextA, ContextB, and ContextC.
