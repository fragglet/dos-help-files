/Er Option

─────BSCMAKE────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     Syntax:  /Er symbol
              /Er (symbol...)
 
     Excludes the specified symbols from the database. To specify
     multiple symbols, separate the names with spaces and enclose the
     list in parentheses.
