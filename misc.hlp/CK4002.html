     CVPACK Warning CK4002
 
     duplicate public symbol 'symbol' in module 'module'
 
     The given symbol was redefined in the given module. CVPACK deleted
     the second occurrence of the symbol.
 
     Probably an earlier version of the linker was used. Use LINK 5.30
     or later.
