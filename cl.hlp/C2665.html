     Compiler error C2665
 
     'function' : 'number1' overloads have no legal conversion for
     parameter 'number2'
 
     The specified parameter of the overloaded function could not be
     converted to the required type.
 
     A conversion operator or explicit conversion may need to be
     supplied.
