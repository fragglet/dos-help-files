     Compiler warning (level 3) C4306
 
     conversion of integral type to pointer of greater size
 
     An integral type was cast to a larger pointer. The unfilled high
     bits of the new type will be zero-filled.
 
     This warning may indicate an unwanted conversion. The resulting
     pointer may not be valid.
