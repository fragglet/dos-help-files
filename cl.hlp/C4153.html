     Compiler warning (level 1) C4153
 
     function/data pointer conversion in expression
 
     A pointer to a function was converted to a pointer to data, or
     visa versa.
 
     This conversion is not legal in ANSI C.
 
     This conversion is allowed when the Microsoft extensions (/Ze)
     are enabled, and causes a level 4 warning.
