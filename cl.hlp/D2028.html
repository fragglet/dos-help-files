     Command-line error D2028
 
     too many open files; cannot redirect 'filename'
 
     Redirection of one of the standard stream files was not possible
     because too many files were already open and a duplicate handle
     could not be created.
 
     To increase the number of file handles available under DOS, change
     the FILES setting in CONFIG.SYS. FILES=50 is the recommended
     setting.
