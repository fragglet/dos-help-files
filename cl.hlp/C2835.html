     Compiler error C2835
 
     user-defined conversion 'type' takes no formal parameters
 
     A user-defined type conversion was declared as having one or
     more formal parameters. User-defined type conversions cannot take
     formal parameters.
 
     Remove the formal parameters or choose an operator to overload.
