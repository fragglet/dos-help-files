     Fatal error C1056
 
     compiler limit : out of macro expansion space
 
     The compiler overflowed an internal buffer during the expansion of
     a macro.
 
     Try to split the macros into simpler macros or remove nonessential
     space and tab characters from macro definitions that were used in
     the expansion.
