     Syntax:  /Od
 
     This option turns off optimization. Because optimization can
     involve rearrangement of instructions, specify the /Od option when
     you plan to use CodeView with your program.
     See: ◄Prepare for Debug (/Zi)►
 
     Turning off or restricting optimization of a program usually
     increases the size of the generated code.
