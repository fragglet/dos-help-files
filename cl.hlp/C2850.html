     Compiler error C2850
 
     #pragma hdrstop cannot be nested in a function or definition
 
     The hdrstop pragma cannot be placed inside the body of a
     function or definition.
 
     Move the #pragma hdrstop statement to an area that is not
     contained in curly braces or parentheses.
