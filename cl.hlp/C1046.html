     Fatal error C1046
 
     compiler limit : 'structure' nested too deeply
 
     The given structure, union, or class exceeded the nesting limit of
     the compiler.
 
     Structures, unions, and classes cannot be nested to more than 15
     levels.
 
     Rewrite the definition so that fewer structures, unions, or classes
     are nested. The structure, union, or class can be split into two or
     more parts by defining one or more of the nested structures using
     typedef.
