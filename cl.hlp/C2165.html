     Compiler error C2165
 
     'keyword' : cannot modify pointers to data
 
     The __fortran, __pascal, __cdecl, or __fastcall keyword was used
     illegally to modify a pointer to data, as in the following
     example:
 
          char __pascal *p;   // error
