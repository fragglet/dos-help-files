     Fatal error C1063
 
     compiler limit : compiler stack overflow
 
     The program was too complex and caused the compiler stack to
     overflow.
 
     Simplify the program, making it more modular, and recompile. If
     the /f or /qc fast-compilation command-line option is being
     used, recompile without it.
