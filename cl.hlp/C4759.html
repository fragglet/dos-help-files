     Compiler warning (level 2) C4759
 
     segment lost in conversion
 
     The conversion of a far pointer (a full segmented address) or
     based pointer to a near pointer (a segment offset) or a different
     based pointer resulted in the loss of the segment address.
 
     This warning is informational.
