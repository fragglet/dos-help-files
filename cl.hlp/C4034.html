     Compiler warning (level 1) C4034
 
     sizeof returns 0
 
     The sizeof operator was applied to an operand that yielded a size
     of zero.
 
     This warning indicates that the operand of the sizeof operator was
     an empty structure, union, class, or enumerated type, or was of
     type void.
