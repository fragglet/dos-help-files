     Compiler error C2583
 
     'identifier' : illegal const/volatile 'this' pointer used for
     constructors/destructors
 
     A constructor or destructor was declared with a const or volatile
     specifier.
 
     The const and volatile declaration specifiers cannot be used for
     constructors or destructors.
