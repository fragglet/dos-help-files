     Compiler warning (level 1) C4342
 
     precision lost in initialization of enum constant
 
     An enumerated constant was given in an expression too complex for
     the compiler to resolve accurately to an integer.
 
     Make sure that the compiler can resolve the constant by simplifying
     the expression.
