     Fatal error C1002
 
     compiler is out of heap space in pass 2
 
     The compiler ran out of dynamic memory space during execution of
     the second pass of the compiler (C2.EXE). Usually this means the
     program has too many symbols and/or complex expressions.
 
     One of the following may be a solution:
 
        ■ Divide the file into several smaller source files.
 
        ■ Break expressions into smaller subexpressions.
 
        ■ Remove other programs or drivers running in the system
          that could be consuming significant amounts of memory.
