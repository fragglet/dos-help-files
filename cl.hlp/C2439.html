     Compiler error C2439
 
     'identifier' : member could not be initialized
 
     The indicated class, structure, or union member could not be
     initialized.
 
     This error can be caused by trying to initialize an indirect base
     class or structure or an inherited member of a class or structure.
     An inherited member should be initialized by the constructor of
     the class or structure.
