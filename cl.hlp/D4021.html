     Command-line warning D4021
 
     no action performed
 
     A contradictory set of filenames and switches caused the compiler
     to perform no operation.
 
     This warning can be generated by giving the /c compile-only
     command-line option and specifying no .C, .CPP, or .CXX files to
     compile.
