     Compiler error C2257
 
     p-code generation pragma not allowed without /Oq
 
     The "q" optimization pragma is not allowed without the /Oq p-code
     command-line option.
 
     To generate p-code from CL, use the /Oq p-code command-line
     option. Once the option has been set, the optimization pragmas
     can turn p-code generation on and off.
