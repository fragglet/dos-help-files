     Compiler error C2856
 
     #pragma hdrstop cannot be inside an #if block
 
     The hdrstop pragma cannot be placed inside the body of a
     conditional compilation block.
 
     Move the #pragma hdrstop statement to an area that is not
     contained in an #if/#endif block.
