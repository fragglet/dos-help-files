     Compiler warning (level 3) C4133
 
     'type' : incompatible types - 'void' pointer combined with
     non-'void' pointer
 
     An attempt was made to subtract two pointers, one of which is a
     pointer to a void type.
 
     This warning can be avoided by providing the appropriate type cast.
