Error Message
 
Error 37:  Integer or real constant expected
 
QuickPascal requires a numeric constant at the cursor.
 
You cannot use a variable or an expression that uses
variables at this location.
 
Insert a new numeric constant or the appropriate existing
numeric constant at the cursor.
