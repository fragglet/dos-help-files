Error Message
 
Error 41:  Low bound exceeds high bound
 
The low bound range in a declared array exceeds the high
bound range. For example:
 
   ARRAY[1..10] OF Integer;    {correct}
   TYPE
       my_rg = 1..10;
 
   ARRAY[10..1] OF Integer;    {incorrect}
   TYPE
       my_rg = 10..1;
