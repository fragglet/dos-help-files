  Message:      0082
 
  Text:         COBOL Division or Section header encountered in
                incorrect order
 
  Explanation:  A COBOL Division or Section header was encountered
                in the wrong order in the source. For example, LINKAGE
                SECTION was encountered after SCREEN SECTION had already
                been read.
