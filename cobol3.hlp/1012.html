  Message:      1012
 
  Text:         DIVISION missing or misspelled.  DIVISION assumed
 
  Explanation:  You have omitted the word DIVISION from a division
                header, or you have spelled it incorrectly.  Your
                COBOL system has assumed that DIVISION is what was
                meant.
