  Message:      024
 
  Text:         Data size incompatible with this model
 
  Severity:     Recoverable
 
  Explanation:  In a 16 bit environment, you have specified MODEL(SMALL)
                or MODEL(MEDIUM) but the Data Division is larger
                than 64K.
 
  Resolution:   Change MODEL(SMALL) to MODEL(COMPACT), or MODEL(MEDIUM)
                to MODEL(LARGE).
