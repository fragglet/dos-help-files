  Message:      RT020
 
  Text:         Device or resource busy
 
  Severity:     Recoverable
 
  Explanation:  You have attempted to open a file that is assigned to
                a device or resource (for example, a line printer)
                that is not available at this time.
 
  Resolution:   You can trap the error status returned by open and
                retry the open at regular intervals until it succeeds.
