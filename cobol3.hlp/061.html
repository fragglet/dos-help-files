  Message:      061
 
  Text:         GANIM not valid with OPT(0). OPT(1) assumed
 
  Severity:     Cautionary
 
  Explanation:  When you use an invalid combination of directives,
                your COBOL system tries to make it valid by assuming
                appropriate directives.
 
  Resolution:   To prevent the message being displayed, use the NOWARNING
                directive.
