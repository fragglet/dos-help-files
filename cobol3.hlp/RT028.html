  Message:      RT028
 
  Text:         No space on device
 
  Severity:     Fatal
 
  Explanation:  You have attempted a file operation such as WRITE for
                which there is not sufficient space available on your
                disk.
 
  Resolution:   When your program has terminated you will have to
                delete some of the files or directories on your
                current logged in disk. Ensure that you delete
                sufficient files on your disk so that you have enough
                room to carry out successful file operations.
