  Message:      1149
 
  Text:         Operand <data-name> ambiguous in corresponding target
 
  Explanation:  In a MOVE, ADD or SUBTRACT CORRESPONDING, the sending
                group contains an item, <data-name>, which is ambiguous in
                the receiving group.
 
  Resolution:   Although <data-name> occurs in both the sending and
                receiving groups, no relevant MOVE, ADD or SUBTRACT is
                generated: this is in accordance with ANSI defined
                behavior.
