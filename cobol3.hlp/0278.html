  Message:      0278
 
  Text:         RESET item is lower CONTROL level than group
 
  Explanation:  The control group on which a sum is reset must be at
                either the same level as or a lower level than the sum.
 
  Resolution:   Check your Language Reference for the rules
                governing the resetting of sums. Revise your code
                accordingly.
