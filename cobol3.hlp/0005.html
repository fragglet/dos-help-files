  Message:      0005
 
  Text:         User-name <user-name> not unique
 
  Explanation:  You have given the same user name without
                qualification to more than one data-item or
                procedure-name in your source code.
 
  Resolution:   You must rename or qualify the duplicated data-items
                or procedure-names to ensure that uniqueness of
                reference is achieved.
