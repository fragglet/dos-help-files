  Message:      RT119
 
  Text:         Symbol redefined
 
  Severity:     Fatal
 
  Explanation:  You are unable to load your object file because it has
                an entry point with the same name as a module already
                loaded.
 
  Resolution:   Once your program has terminated recode it to remove
                the naming duplication.  Resubmit your program to your
                COBOL system.
