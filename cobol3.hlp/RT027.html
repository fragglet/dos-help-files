  Message:      RT027
 
  Text:         Device not available
 
  Severity:     Recoverable
 
  Explanation:  You are attempting to access a device which either is
                not attached to your machine or if attached is not
                on-line.
 
  Resolution:   Attach the device to your machine and ensure that it
                is on-line.  Repeat the file operation.
