  Message:      RT168
 
  Text:         Stack overflow
 
  Severity:     Fatal
 
  Explanation:  You have nested a PERFORM statement or a series of
                CALL statements too deeply.
 
  Resolution:   Edit your program to reduce the number of levels
                within a nested PERFORM or CALL statement, then
                resubmit your source code to your COBOL system.
