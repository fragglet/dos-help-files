  Message:      0122
 
  Text:         Cannot use Double Byte characters in alphabet or class
                definition
 
  Explanation:  When defining an alphabet or class, you may not use
                double-byte characters.
 
  Resolution:   Redesign your program so that your alphabet or class
                definition does not include any double-byte characters.
