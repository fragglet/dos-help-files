  Message:      RT005
 
  Text:         Illegal device specification
 
  Severity:     Recoverable
 
  Explanation:  Devices to which your COBOL program can write are
                defined by the operating system. You have tried to
                write to a device that is not defined by your system.
 
  Resolution:   Try the operation again using a device name that your
                system recognizes.
