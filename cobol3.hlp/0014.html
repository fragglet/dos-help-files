  Message:      0014
 
  Text:         Invalid operand
 
  Explanation:  The operand you have specified is in some way
                incorrect, and cannot be processed by your COBOL
                system. For example, you may have specified a negative
                integer where only positive integers are allowed.
 
  Resolution:   Refer to your Language Reference for details of
                the operands allowed for this syntax.
