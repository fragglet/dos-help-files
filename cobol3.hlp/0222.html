  Message:      0222
 
  Text:         REDEFINES data-name not declared
 
  Explanation:  You have not declared the data name which you have
                used in conjunction with a REDEFINES clause.
 
  Resolution:   Ensure that the data name is declared in your Data
                Description entry and that the relevant REDEFINES
                clause is placed immediately after the data name which
                it describes.
