  Message:      1126
 
  Text:         Punctuation character not preceded by a space. Assume
                space
 
  Explanation:  In accordance with ANS85 specifications, the relational
                operator "=" is considered to be a COBOL reserved word; all
                reserved words must be both preceded and delimited by a
                space.
