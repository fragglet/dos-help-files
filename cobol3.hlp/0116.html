  Message:      0116
 
  Text:         Character specified twice in alphabet
 
  Explanation:  You have specified at least one character twice in the
                ALPHABET clause.  For example, you may have included a
                character in a range and also specified it as a
                literal.
 
  Resolution:   Edit your source code so that the characters specified
                in the ALPHABET clause are referenced only once.
