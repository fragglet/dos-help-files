  Message:      RT108
 
  Text:         Failure to initialize data division
 
  Severity:     Fatal
 
  Explanation:  The run-time system cannot load your program properly
                because the data needed to correctly initialize the
                Data Divison has become corrupted.
 
  Resolution:   You should compile your program again to try to obtain
                good intermediate code.
