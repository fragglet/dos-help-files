  Message:      0320
 
  Text:         Too many operands in one statement
 
  Explanation:  A statement in the Procedure Division of your program
                contains too many operands or individual operands that are
                too lengthy.
 
  Resolution:   Reduce the number of operands, or shorten those
                operands that are too lengthy.
