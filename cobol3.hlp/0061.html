  Message:      0061
 
  Text:         Pseudo text incorrectly specified
 
  Explanation:  A two-character delimiter for pseudotext is missing
                from either a REPLACE or COPY REPLACING statement.
 
  Resolution:   Insert the missing pseudotext delimiter (that is, ==)
                at the appropriate point in your program.
