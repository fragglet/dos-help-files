  Message:      057
 
  Text:         NOLITLINK not valid with MODEL(SMALL), LITLINK assumed
 
  Severity:     Cautionary
 
  Explanation:  When you use an invalid combination of directives, your
                COBOL system tries to make it valid by assuming appropriate
                directives.
 
  Resolution:   To prevent the message being displayed, use the NOWARNING
                directive.
