  Message:      0048
 
  Text:         Missing or extra right parenthesis
 
  Explanation:  The number of left and right parentheses in an
                arithmetic expression is not the same.
 
  Resolution:   Check the format of the arithmetic expression and
                ensure that there is a matching right parenthesis for
                each left parenthesis.
