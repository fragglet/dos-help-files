  Message:      RT207
 
  Text:         Machine does not exist
 
  Severity:     Recoverable
 
  Explanation:  You have tried to access a machine that is not
                connected to your network, or which is not on line.
 
  Resolution:   Make sure the machine is connected to the network and
                is on-line, then attempt to access it again.
