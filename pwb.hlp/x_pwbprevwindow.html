     _pwbprevwindow («_pwbprevwindow»)
 
     The _pwbprevwindow macro moves the focus to the previous window.
     That is, PWB sets the previously active window as the active
     window. This is action moves among the open windows in the reverse
     order of Selwindow («selwindow»).
