     Save Editor Settings
 
     Choose Save Editor Settings to save new switch settings
     permanently.
 
     The Modified Switches List box shows all the unsaved switch
     assignments made by in the Editor Settings dialog box during the
     session.
 
     To save all new settings:
 
       ■  Choose OK.
 
     To save only some of the new settings:
 
       1. Select the setting you do not want to save.
 
       2. Choose Delete.
 
          Repeat steps 1 and 2 above for each setting that you do not
          want to save.
 
       3. Choose OK.
 
     When you choose OK, PWB modifies the [PWB] section of your
     TOOLS.INI file to reflect the new switch settings. When you exit
     PWB, you are prompted to save TOOLS.INI. Your settings are not
     permanent until you actually save TOOLS.INI to disk.
 
     NOTE: If you started PWB with the /D or /DT (disable TOOLS.INI)
           command-line options, you cannot save new settings.
