     Project menu, Cancel Build command («_pwbcancelbuild»)
 
     Macro  ◄_pwbcancelbuild►
 
     Choose Cancel Build to stop the current background build or
     compile operation. PWB stops all queued background operations.
 
     To stop a foreground build or compile operation, press CTRL+C.
 
     To stop scanning the Build Results, press CTRL+BREAK.
