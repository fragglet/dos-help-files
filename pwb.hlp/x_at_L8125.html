     _pwbquit := cancel arg exit <
 
     Cancel      Establishes a uniform "ground state" by canceling any
                 selection or argument.
 
     Arg Exit    Leaves PWB.
 
     <           Restores the function's prompt (if any). By default,
                 function prompts are suppressed while a macro is
                 running.
