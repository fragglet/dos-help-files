     <RECORD>Record
 
     The <RECORD> pseudofile contains the most recently recorded macro.
 
     You can open this file by opening the Window menu and choosing
     Record from the PWB windows cascaded menu. You can also choose
     Record in the All Files dialog box.
 
     When you save the <RECORD> pseudofile, PWB writes the macro
     definition and associated key (if any) to your TOOLS.INI file. The
     change is not permanent until you save TOOLS.INI.
 
     See
 
     Pwbwindow, Record
