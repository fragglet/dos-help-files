     Update: Predefined Macros
 
     Restart
          With PWB 1.x, the Restart macro is executed whenever PWB
          returns from a shell, build, or other external operation with
          DOS. Because PWB 2.00 retains its complete internal state
          across external operations, this macro is not necessary and
          is ignored.
