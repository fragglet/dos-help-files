     Update: Text Cursor
 
     With PWB 1.x, the cursor is always an underscore.
 
     With PWB 2.00, the cursor changes from a block to an underscore
     depending on insert or overtype mode, according to the setting of
     the Cursormode switch. By default, the cursor is a block in insert
     mode and an underscore in overtype mode. See: Cursormode
