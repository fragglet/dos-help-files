     The following lists PWB's special pseudofiles. For more
     information on a pseudofile listed below, double-click the name
     or move the cursor to the name and press ENTER.
 
     PWB Pseudofiles
 
     <CLIPBOARD>Clipboard
     "<COMPILE>Build Results"
     "<FILE-LIST>Next Files To Edit" *
     "<PRINT>Print Results"
     <RECORD>Record
     "<SEARCH>Search Results"
 
     Extension Pseudofiles
 
     <pwbhelp>Help
     "<browse>Browser Output"
     "<brmsg>Browser Message Line" *
 
     Obsolete Pseudofiles
 
     "<ASSIGN>Current Assignments and Switch Settings"
     "<STATUS-FILE>PWB Status File"
     <INFORMATION-FILE>
     <WHERE>
 
     * Pseudofile does not have a corresponding PWB window.
