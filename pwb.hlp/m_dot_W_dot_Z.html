     Window menu, Minimize command («_pwbminimize»)
 
     Macro  ◄_pwbminimize►
 
     Choose Minimize to shrink the active window to an icon. An icon
     looks like this:
 
            ┌─────┐
            │  1  │
            └─────┘
          filename.ext
 
     To restore a window to its original size, double-click the icon or
     move the focus to the window and press ENTER or choose Window
     Restore («_pwbrestore»).
     See: ◄Window menu, Restore►
 
     You can also minimize a window by clicking the down-pointing arrow
     in the upper-right border of the window.
     See: ◄PWB Window Diagram►
 
     An icon is not affected by the Cascade, Tile, or Arrange commands.
