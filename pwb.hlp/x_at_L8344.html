     Update: Pseudofiles
 
     The following pseudofiles in PWB 2.00 do not exist in PWB 1.x:
 
          "<SEARCH>Search Results"
          "<brmsg>Browser Message Line"
 
     The following pseudofiles in PWB 1.x are obsolete and do not exist
     in PWB 2.00:
 
          "<ASSIGN>Current Assignments and Switch Settings"
          <INFORMATION-FILE>
          "<STATUS-FILE>PWB Status File"
          "<WHERE>File Search Results"
