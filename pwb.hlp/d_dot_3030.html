─────Programmer's WorkBench─────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     PWB is prompting for confirmation to replace the highlighted
     occurrence of the find text or pattern.
 
     Choose one of the following actions:
 
     Option         Action
 
     Yes            Replaces the highlighted text and continues.
 
     No             Skips the highlighted text and continues.
 
     Replace All    Replaces the highlighted text and continues to
                    replace all occurrences without prompting for
                    confirmation.
 
     Cancel         Does not replace the highlighted text and stops the
                    search.
 
     Help           Displays this Help.
