     Plines («plines»)
          Scrolls the text up as specified by the Vscroll switch.
          See: Vscroll
 
     Arg Plines («arg» «plines»)
          Scrolls the text such that the line at the cursor is moved to
          the top of the window.
 
     Arg <number> Plines («arg» <number> «plines»)
          Scrolls the text up by <number> lines.
 
     Returns
 
     True:  Text scrolled.
     False: Invalid argument.
 
     See
 
     Mlines
