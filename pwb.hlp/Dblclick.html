     Dblclick (Numeric)
 
     The Dblclick switch sets the double-click threshold for the mouse
     (the maximum time between successive clicks of the mouse button).
     The units for the Dblclick switch are 1/18 of a second.
 
     Default
 
     Dblclick:10
 
     See
 
     Mousemode
