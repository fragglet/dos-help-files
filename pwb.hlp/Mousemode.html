     Mousemode (Numeric)
 
     The Mousemode switch enables or disables the mouse, and sets the
     actions of the left and right mouse buttons.
 
     Value     Description
 
     0         The mouse is disabled, and the mouse pointer is not
               visible.
 
     1         Normal mouse control.
 
     2         Exchanges the actions of the left and right mouse
               buttons. Left-handed people usually prefer this setting.
 
     Default
 
     Mousemode:1
