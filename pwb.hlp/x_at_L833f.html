     Update: Environment Variables
 
     With PWB 1.x, the INCLUDE, LIB, and HELPFILES environment
     variables are always saved for PWB sessions and projects. With PWB
     2.00, the entire environment can be saved for PWB sessions and/or
     projects, or PWB can simply use the current operating-system
     environment.
     See: Envcursave, Envprojsave
