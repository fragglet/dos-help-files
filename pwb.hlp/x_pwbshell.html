     _pwbshell («_pwbshell»)
 
     Menu  ◄File OS/2 Shell►
           ◄File menu, DOS Shell►
 
     The _pwbshell macro temporarily leaves PWB and starts a new
     operating-system shell. To return to PWB, type 'Exit' at the
     operating-system prompt.
