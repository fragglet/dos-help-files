─────Programmer's WorkBench─────────────────────────────────────────────────
 
     The PWB macro language is a simple language without types,
     declarations, arithmetic, or variables. This seems limited at
     first, but with regular-expression searching, flow control, and the
     complete set of PWB functions, the language is surprisingly
     powerful.
