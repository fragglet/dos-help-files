     Savescreen (Boolean)
 
     The Savescreen switch determines if PWB preserves the operating-
     system screen image and video mode.
 
     Syntax
 
     Savescreen:{ yes | no }
 
     yes    Save the operating-system screen when starting PWB, and
            restore it when leaving PWB.
 
     no     Do not preserve the operating-system screen.
 
            When you leave PWB, the operating-system screen is blank
            and the video mode is left in PWB's last video mode.
 
     Default
 
     Savescreen:yes
