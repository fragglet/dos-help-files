     Color (Text)
 
     The Color switch specifies color of various parts of the PWB
     display.
 
     Syntax
 
     Color:<name> <value>
 
     <name>    Identifies the part of PWB affected by the color value.
               See: ◄Color Names►
 
     <value>   Two hexadecimal digits specifying the foreground and
               background color of the indicated item.
               See: ◄Color Values►
