     Restart (obsolete)
 
     Update
 
     In PWB 1.x, the special PWB macro Restart is executed whenever PWB
     returns from a shell, build, or other external operation.
 
     In PWB 2.00, the Restart macro is never executed automatically and
     has no special meaning──it is an ordinary macro.
