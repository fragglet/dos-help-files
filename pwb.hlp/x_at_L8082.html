     Update: Mgrep
 
     In PWB 2.00, search and build results and their browsing functions
     are separate. A background build operation and a background search
     can be performed simultaneously.
 
     In PWB 1.x, search and build results appear in the same window,
     and are browsed with the same commands. A background build
     operation and a multifile search cannot be performed at the same
     time in PWB 1.x.
