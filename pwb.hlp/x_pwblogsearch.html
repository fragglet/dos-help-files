     _pwblogsearch («_pwblogsearch»)
 
     Menu  ◄Search menu, Log►
 
     The _pwblogsearch macro toggles search logging on and off.
 
     When search logging is on, PWB displays a bullet next to Log on
     the Search menu. Next Match executes the _pwbnextlogmatch
     macro, and Previous Match executes the _pwbpreviouslogmatch
     macro. When logging is off, no bullet appears and the Next
     Match and Previous Match commands execute _pwbnextmatch and
     _pwbpreviousmatch.
     See: _pwbnextlogmatch, _pwbnextmatch, _pwbpreviouslogmatch,
          _pwbpreviousmatch
