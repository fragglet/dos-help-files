     <CLIPBOARD>Clipboard
 
     The <CLIPBOARD> pseudofile is the PWB clipboard.
 
     You can open this file by opening the Window menu and choosing
     Clipboard from the PWB Windows cascaded menu. You can also choose
     Clipboard in the All Files dialog box.
