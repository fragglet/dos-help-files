     Edit menu, Delete command («_pwbclear»)
 
     Macro  ◄_pwbclear►
 
     Choose Delete to clear the selected text from the file. The
     selection is not copied to the clipboard. If no text is selected,
     PWB deletes the character at the cursor.
 
     TIP: To view the contents of the clipboard, choose Clipboard from
          the PWB Windows cascaded menu on the Window menu.
 
     See
