     Menukey («menukey»)
 
     Activates the menu bar. Unlike other PWB functions, Menukey can be
     assigned to only one key.
 
     NOTE: Menukey cannot be assigned to a combination of keys.
 
     Returns
 
     You cannot test the return value of Menukey.
