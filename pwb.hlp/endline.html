     Endline («endline»)
          Moves the cursor to the immediate right of the last character
          on the line.
 
     Meta Endline («meta» «endline»)
          Moves the cursor to the column that is one column past the
          active window width.
 
     Returns
 
     True:  Cursor moved.
     False: Cursor did not move; the cursor is at the destination.
 
     See
 
     Begline, Traildisp, Trailspace
