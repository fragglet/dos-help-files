     Update: Inserting ASCII Characters
 
     With PWB 1.x, ALT+<keypad digits> works independently of the
     numlock state. This disallows the use of ALT in combination with
     the cursor-movement keys on the keypad. To enable these
     combinations, PWB 2.00 requires numlock to be turned on to enter
     the ASCII digits.
