     Hscroll (Numeric)
 
     The Hscroll switch controls the number of columns that PWB scrolls
     the text left or right when the you move the cursor out of the
     window. When the window does not occupy the full screen, the
     amount scrolled is in proportion to the window size.
 
     Text is never scrolled in increments greater than the size of the
     window.
 
     Default
 
     Hscroll:10
