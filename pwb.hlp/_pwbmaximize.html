     _pwbmaximize («_pwbmaximize»)
 
     Menu  ◄Window menu, Maximize►
 
     The _pwbmaximize macro enlarges the active window to its largest
     possible size, showing only the window, the menu bar, and the
     status bar on the PWB screen.
