     Rmargin
 
     In PWB 1.x, Rmargin sets the beginning of a six-character
     "probation" zone where typing a space wraps the line. After the
     zone, typing any character wraps the current word. This behavior
     is similar to that of a typewriter. PWB 2.0 uses a word-
     processor's style of wrapping.
 
     To maintain the same margins as PWB 1.x, increase your Rmargin
     settings by 6.
