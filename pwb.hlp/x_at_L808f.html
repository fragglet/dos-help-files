     Update: Nextmsg
 
     In PWB 1.x, Nextmsg also browses the results of searches. In PWB
     2.00, search results are browsed with the Nextsearch function.
     See: Nextsearch
 
     Meta Nextmsg
          In PWB 1.x, deletes the current set of messages and advances
          to the next set. In PWB 2.00, Meta Nextmsg does not delete
          the set. To delete sets of messages in PWB 2.00, use the
          Clearmsg function.
 
     Meta Arg Arg Nextmsg
          In PWB 1.x, closes the Compile Results window. In PWB 2.00,
          it behaves like Arg Arg Nextmsg.
