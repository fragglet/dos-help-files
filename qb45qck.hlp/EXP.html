EXP - a math function that calculates the exponential function
      (e raised to the power of a numeric expression).
 
Syntax
  EXP(numeric-expression)
    ■ numeric-expression, which must have a value less than or equal to
      88.02969, is the power to which e, the base of natural logarithms,
      is to be raised.
