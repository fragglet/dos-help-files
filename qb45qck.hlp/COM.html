COM(n) - an event trapping statement that enables, disables, or inhibits
         event trapping of communications activity on a specified port
 
Syntax
  COM(n) ON
  COM(n) OFF
  COM(n) STOP
 
    ■ n is the number of the communications port, either 1 or 2
