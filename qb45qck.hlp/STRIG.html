STRIG(n) - an event-trapping statement that enables, disables, or
           inhibits trapping of joystick activity
Syntax
  STRIG(n) ON
  STRIG(n) OFF
  STRIG(n) STOP
 
See Also  ◄ON event statements►
Other Uses of the STRIG Keyword
  ◄STRIG function► - to return the status of a specified joystick trigger
