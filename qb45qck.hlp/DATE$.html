DATE$ - a statement that uses DOS to set the current date
 
Syntax
  DATE$ = stringexpression
    ■ stringexpression must have one of the following forms:
        mm-dd-yy, mm-dd-yyyy, mm/dd/yy, or mm/dd/yyyy
      where mm and dd are the month and day, and yy or yyyy are the year
